This field encompasses a wide range of tools, software, and hardware designed to enhance instruction, facilitate student engagement, and streamline educational administration. It aims to make learning more accessible, personalized, and effective for individuals of all ages and backgrounds.

For instance, a teacher might use an online platform for delivering assignments and tracking student progress.

EdTech is commonly discussed and implemented within academic institutions, corporate training departments, and by educational technology companies.
