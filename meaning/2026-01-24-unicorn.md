The unicorn is a legendary animal depicted in various ancient texts and folklore. Its most defining characteristic is the prominent horn, often described as being long, pointed, and twisted. These creatures are typically portrayed as pure, gentle, and elusive, often associated with healing powers and a strong connection to nature. Legends suggest that only the virtuous or pure of heart could approach or capture a unicorn.

*Example:* "The knight's quest was to find the legendary unicorn, whose horn was said to possess miraculous healing properties."

The concept of the unicorn appears in the art, literature, and mythology of many cultures, dating back to antiquity. It is a popular motif in fantasy literature, children's stories, and various forms of visual art.
