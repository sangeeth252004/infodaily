This method of instruction utilizes computers, the internet, and digital devices to present educational content and facilitate learning. It encompasses a wide range of formats, from structured courses with video lectures and interactive quizzes to simpler resources like informational websites and educational apps. Learners can often access e-learning materials at their own pace and from any location with internet access, offering flexibility.

For instance, a student might complete an entire university degree program through e-learning modules and online lectures.

This term is commonly found in contexts related to education, professional development, corporate training, and academic institutions.
