Digital pedagogy examines how educators can effectively integrate digital tools, platforms, and resources into their instructional methods to enhance student engagement and learning outcomes. This involves understanding the design of learning experiences, the selection of appropriate technologies, and the facilitation of learning in digital environments.

For instance, a teacher might use online collaborative documents to facilitate group projects, allowing students to work together in real-time and receive immediate feedback.

The term is frequently encountered in the fields of education, educational technology, and instructional design.
