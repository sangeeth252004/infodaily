This involves acquiring knowledge and abilities that are more advanced or specialized than those currently possessed. It is often undertaken to remain competitive in a rapidly evolving job market or to adapt to technological advancements.

For example, a marketing professional might pursue upskilling in digital analytics to better understand campaign performance.

This term is commonly used in discussions related to career development, employee training, and workforce preparedness within the business and education sectors.
