This involves developing and sharing various types of information, such as blog posts, articles, videos, infographics, podcasts, and social media updates. The goal is to educate, inform, or entertain the target audience, establishing credibility and trust with them. By consistently providing useful content, businesses aim to build relationships and encourage engagement, leading to desired outcomes like website visits, lead generation, or sales.

*For example, a software company might publish a series of tutorials demonstrating how to use their product's features to solve common industry problems.*

This practice is widely employed in digital marketing, inbound marketing, and brand building efforts across many industries.
