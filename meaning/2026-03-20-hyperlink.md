A hyperlink connects one digital document or resource to another. When you interact with a hyperlink, it directs you to a different location within the same document, a separate document, a specific webpage on the internet, or even a downloadable file. These connections are fundamental to navigating the vast landscape of digital information.

For instance, clicking on the underlined text "learn more" might take you to a different webpage with additional details.

Hyperlinks are most commonly encountered on websites and within digital documents such as emails and word processing files.
