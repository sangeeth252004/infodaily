This involves a range of technologies, processes, and practices designed to safeguard data, devices, and infrastructure from unauthorized access, damage, or theft. The goal is to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information and systems.

For instance, a company might implement strong password policies and regular software updates as part of its cybersecurity strategy to prevent unauthorized access.

The term is frequently encountered in discussions related to information technology, national security, business operations, and personal data protection.
