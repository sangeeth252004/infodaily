This type of software provides a user-friendly interface, often with graphical tools, that allows individuals to add, edit, and organize text, images, videos, and other digital assets. It separates the content from its presentation, meaning changes to the content do not require altering the website's underlying code.

For example, a blogger could use a content management system to write and publish a new article, upload accompanying photos, and schedule its release date.

Content management systems are widely employed in web development, digital marketing, and corporate communications for building and maintaining websites, intranets, and digital archives.
