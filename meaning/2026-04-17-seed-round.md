This initial investment provides capital for a startup to begin operations, develop its product or service, and establish a market presence. Funds from a seed round are typically used for research and development, initial marketing efforts, and hiring essential personnel. It is a crucial step for businesses transitioning from an idea to a functioning enterprise.

Example: The company successfully closed its seed round, securing the necessary funds to launch its beta product.

This term is commonly used in the context of venture capital and startup finance.
