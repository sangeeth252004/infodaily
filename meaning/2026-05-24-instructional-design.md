Instructional design involves analyzing the needs of learners, defining learning objectives, developing content and activities, implementing instruction, and evaluating its effectiveness. It focuses on making learning efficient, engaging, and impactful by structuring information and activities in a logical and accessible manner.

For instance, a company might use instructional design principles to develop a training program for new employees, ensuring they quickly understand their roles and responsibilities.

This practice is commonly found in education (from K-12 to higher education), corporate training, e-learning platforms, and military or government training programs.
