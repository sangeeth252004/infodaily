A curriculum serves as a roadmap for education, detailing what knowledge and skills students are expected to acquire at different stages of their schooling. It encompasses the selection of topics, the order in which they are presented, and the methods by which learning is assessed. This plan guides educators in their teaching practices and helps ensure a consistent educational experience for students.

For instance, a high school mathematics curriculum might specify the progression from algebra to geometry and then to calculus, along with the specific concepts to be covered in each.

The term curriculum is primarily used in educational settings, including schools, universities, and training programs.
