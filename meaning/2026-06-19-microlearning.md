This approach breaks down complex subjects into easily digestible modules, often lasting only a few minutes. Each unit concentrates on a specific skill or piece of information, making it efficient for learners to acquire knowledge.

For instance, an employee might complete a five-minute video on proper email etiquette.

Microlearning is frequently employed in corporate training, professional development, and educational settings seeking to provide flexible and accessible learning opportunities.
