The Republic Day Parade flypast is a key feature of India's national celebration, commemorating the date the Constitution of India came into effect. The 2022 flypast was particularly notable, featuring a grand formation of 75 aircraft to mark 75 years of India's independence, an 'Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav' celebration. Aircraft types participating in the display commonly include a diverse range, from fighter jets like the Rafale and Sukhoi Su-30MKI to transport aircraft such as the C-130J Super Hercules and C-17 Globemaster III, alongside helicopters like the Apache and Mi-17V5. The released videos showcased segments from these diverse formations, offering distinct perspectives on their operational execution.
