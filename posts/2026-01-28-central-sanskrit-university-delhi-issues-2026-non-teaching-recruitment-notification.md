Central Sanskrit University (CSU) Delhi has officially released a recruitment notification for various non-teaching posts scheduled for the year 2026. The comprehensive advertisement, detailing positions, eligibility criteria, and application procedures, is now accessible on the university's official website, sanskrit.nic.in. This announcement marks the commencement of a significant hiring initiative aimed at bolstering the institution's operational and administrative framework.

The recruitment drive seeks to fill a range of essential roles that are critical for the smooth functioning of the university, supporting its academic mission and infrastructure. These non-teaching positions contribute to areas such as administration, technical support, library services, and general institutional management, which are vital for maintaining an effective learning and research environment.

According to the official notification available on sanskrit.nic.in, prospective applicants are invited to apply for approximately 180 vacancies across different categories. Specific posts include, but are not limited to, Lower Division Clerk (LDC), Multi-Tasking Staff (MTS), Junior Professional Assistant, Laboratory Assistant, and various other administrative and technical support roles. Each position has distinct requirements for educational qualifications, experience, and age limits, all of which are detailed in the full notification.

The application window for these positions is slated to open on October 15, 2024, with the deadline for submissions set for November 15, 2024. All applications must be submitted online exclusively through the designated portal on the sanskrit.nic.in website. Candidates are strongly advised to thoroughly review all sections of the official notification before initiating the application process to ensure compliance with all specified guidelines and requirements.

Key details from the notification include:
*   **Total Vacancies**: Approximately 180 non-teaching posts across various departments.
*   **Eligibility Criteria**: Varies by post, encompassing specific educational qualifications, professional experience, and age limits. Age relaxations will be applicable for reserved categories as per government norms.
*   **Application Process**: Online submission via sanskrit.nic.in.
*   **Application Fee**: A non-refundable application fee is required, payable through online modes.
*   **Selection Process**: Expected to include a multi-stage selection process, which may involve a written examination, skill test, and/or interview, depending on the nature of the post.

This recruitment initiative aligns with Central Sanskrit University Delhi's ongoing efforts to enhance its institutional capacity and ensure robust support for its academic and research programs. The university, recognized for its commitment to Sanskrit studies, aims to attract qualified individuals to contribute to its growth and operational excellence.

Interested and eligible candidates are advised to regularly check the official website, sanskrit.nic.in, for any updates or additional information regarding the application process, examination schedule, and selection procedures. Further communications concerning the next stages of the recruitment are expected to be released following the conclusion of the application period.
