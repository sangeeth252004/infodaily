Marsh, 34, has previously led Australia in T20 Internationals and brings extensive experience to the role. The squad, selected by a panel comprising George Bailey, Tony Dodemaide, and head coach Andrew McDonald, features a blend of seasoned veterans and emerging talent, aiming for a strong performance on the world stage.

Matthew Short's all-round capabilities and Adam Zampa's leg-spin will be crucial in the middle overs.
