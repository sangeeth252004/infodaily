However, the MEA's prompt and categorical denial aimed to set the record straight on the matter of diplomatic engagements.

This direct statement from the official government channels served to dismiss the claims and provide clarity regarding the movements and meetings of senior Indian officials. The specificity of the denial underscored the government's stance against what it deemed inaccurate reporting of sensitive diplomatic exchanges.
