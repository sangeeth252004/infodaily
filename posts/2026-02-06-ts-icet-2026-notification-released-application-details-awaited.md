The comprehensive document, which outlines the eligibility criteria, application process, key dates, and examination pattern for admission into Master of Business Administration (MBA) and Master of Computer Applications (MCA) courses, is now accessible for download on the official website, icet.tgche.ac.in.

The TS ICET is a state-level entrance examination conducted annually on behalf of TSCHE by Kakatiya University, Warangal. It serves as a mandatory gateway for eligible candidates seeking postgraduate degrees in management and computer applications across various universities and affiliated colleges in Telangana. The release of the 2026 notification marks the commencement of the admission cycle for the upcoming academic year, prompting aspiring students to review the detailed guidelines.

According to the newly released notification, the online application process for TS ICET 2026 is expected to commence in the first quarter of 2026. Prospective candidates are advised to meticulously review the official document to ensure they meet all specified eligibility requirements before applying. The examination itself is typically conducted in the second quarter of the year, with specific dates for registration, admit card download, and the test itself detailed within the notification.

Key details highlighted in the TS ICET 2026 notification include:

*   **Conducting Body:** Kakatiya University, Warangal, on behalf of TSCHE.
*   **Purpose:** Admission to MBA and MCA courses in Telangana universities and affiliated colleges.
*   **Eligibility Criteria:** Generally, candidates must hold a bachelor's degree of at least three years' duration from a recognized university, with a minimum aggregate percentage (typically 50% for unreserved categories and 45% for reserved categories). Specific degree requirements vary for MBA and MCA.
*   **Application Process:** Online submission via the official website, icet.tgche.ac.in. This involves registration, fee payment, filling out the application form, and uploading necessary documents.
*   **Examination Pattern:** The TS ICET is a computer-based test (CBT) conducted in English and Telugu. It comprises 200 multiple-choice questions across three sections:
    *   **Analytical Ability:** (75 questions, 75 marks)
    *   **Mathematical Ability:** (75 questions, 75 marks)
    *   **Communication Ability:** (50 questions, 50 marks)
    The total duration of the examination is 150 minutes (2 hours 30 minutes).

The notification also provides comprehensive information regarding the application fee, reservation policies, list of test centers, and the detailed syllabus for each section of the examination.
