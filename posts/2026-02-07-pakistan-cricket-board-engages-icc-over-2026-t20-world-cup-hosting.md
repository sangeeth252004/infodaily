This arrangement was intended to facilitate the staging of a major global cricket tournament across both nations. However, the PCB had subsequently expressed reservations, reportedly linking its participation in co-hosting the 2026 event to resolution of outstanding issues, particularly concerning India's willingness to play matches in Pakistan for ICC tournaments.
