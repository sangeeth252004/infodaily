*   **Investment Protection:** Provisions designed to protect and promote bilateral investments, offering greater certainty for businesses.
*   **Regulatory Cooperation:** Commitments to greater regulatory transparency and cooperation, simplifying trade procedures.

This positive sentiment aligns with the government's broader strategy to integrate India more deeply into global supply chains and enhance its position as a global manufacturing hub.
