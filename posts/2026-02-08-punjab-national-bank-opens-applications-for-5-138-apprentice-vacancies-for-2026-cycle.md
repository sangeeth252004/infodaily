Punjab National Bank (PNB) has officially commenced the application registration process for 5,138 apprentice vacancies, marking a substantial recruitment initiative for its 2026 cycle. Prospective candidates interested in a career in the banking sector can now apply for these positions through the bank's official online portal. The announcement, released recently, details the commencement of the application window, inviting eligible individuals to participate in the competitive selection process.

This recruitment drive is part of PNB's ongoing efforts to enhance its workforce and develop a skilled talent pool. The apprentice program is designed to provide hands-on experience and foundational training in various aspects of banking operations, preparing candidates for future roles within the financial industry. The large number of vacancies, totaling 5,138, indicates a significant opportunity for job seekers, particularly recent graduates looking to enter the banking sector. The apprenticeships typically involve a fixed-term training period, during which individuals gain practical knowledge under the guidance of experienced professionals.

Key details regarding the PNB Apprentice Recruitment 2026 include:

*   **Total Vacancies:** 5,138 apprentice positions across various branches and offices of Punjab National Bank.
*   **Application Start Date:** Applications opened in early December 2023.
*   **Application End Date:** The deadline for submitting online applications is anticipated to be in late December 2023 or early January 2024. Exact dates are specified in the official notification.
*   **Eligibility Criteria (General):**
    *   **Educational Qualification:** Candidates are typically required to hold a bachelor's degree in any discipline from a recognized university or institution.
    *   **Age Limit:** Specific age ranges apply, with relaxations for reserved categories as per government norms. The minimum age is generally 20 years, and the maximum is 28 years.
*   **Selection Process:** The selection methodology is expected to involve multiple stages, designed to assess candidates' aptitude and suitability for the role.
    *   **Online Written Examination:** An online test covering subjects like Reasoning, English Language, Quantitative Aptitude, and General Awareness may be conducted.
    *   **Document Verification:** Shortlisted candidates will undergo a thorough verification of their academic and personal documents.
    *   **Medical Examination:** A final medical examination may be required.
*   **Stipend:** Apprentices selected for the program will receive a monthly stipend during their training period, as per the Apprentices Act and PNB's guidelines.
*   **Training Duration:** The apprenticeship period is generally for 12 months, providing comprehensive exposure to banking operations.

Candidates are strongly advised to refer to the comprehensive official notification released by Punjab National Bank on its corporate website for precise details regarding eligibility, application procedures, examination syllabus, and important dates. The application process is exclusively online, requiring applicants to register, fill out the form, upload necessary documents, and pay the applicable application fee within the stipulated timeframe. This recruitment drive offers a structured pathway for aspiring banking professionals to gain valuable experience and establish a career foundation within one of India's prominent public sector banks.
