The incident, details of which are still emerging from Canadian authorities, unfolded at a school in British Columbia, causing widespread grief and concern. Prime Minister Modi characterized the event as deeply disturbing, emphasizing the gravity of the loss of life and the impact on the community. His statement underscored India's support for Canada during this difficult period, reflecting the strong bilateral ties between the two countries.

In his message, Prime Minister Modi stated he was "deeply shocked" by the "horrendous" nature of the attack. He extended his thoughts and prayers to the victims, their families, and the people of Canada. Such diplomatic expressions of sympathy are customary following major international tragedies, serving to acknowledge shared human sorrow and reinforce diplomatic relations.

The Canadian government and local law enforcement agencies have initiated a comprehensive investigation into the shooting. Authorities are focused on understanding the full scope of the incident, identifying those responsible, and ensuring justice for the victims. The swift response from emergency services was noted, as efforts were made to secure the scene and provide immediate aid to the injured.
