His return to the country in early April, following a period of self-imposed exile, preceded a robust election campaign that culminated in his party securing a majority in the April 28 general elections.

*   **Social Welfare:** Plans to expand social safety nets, improve access to education and healthcare, and create employment opportunities for youth.
