The BJP's demonstrations included chanting "Traitor Rahul," pointing to a significant political escalation surrounding technological discourse.

The protests emerged in the wake of the reported disruption, which took place during an AI conference focused on technological advancements and innovation. The specific details surrounding the initial incident at the AI event, including the exact date and location, were not immediately specified in the provided context, but the BJP's reaction underscored a deep political divide.

*   **Disruption at AI Event:** Members of the Indian National Congress reportedly engaged in an unusual "shirt-off" protest during an AI-focused conference. This act deviated significantly from the typical proceedings of technology-centric gatherings, which usually focus on presentations, discussions, and networking related to emerging technologies.
*   **BJP's Reaction:** The Bharatiya Janata Party promptly condemned the disruption, viewing it as an attempt to undermine the credibility and focus of an important technological forum.
*   **"Traitor Rahul" Slogan:** During their subsequent protests, BJP members were heard chanting "Traitor Rahul." This slogan directly targets Rahul Gandhi, a prominent leader of the Congress party, indicating the BJP's belief that his leadership or the actions of his party were detrimental or disloyal. The use of such a strong epithet highlights the acrimonious relationship between the two major political parties.
