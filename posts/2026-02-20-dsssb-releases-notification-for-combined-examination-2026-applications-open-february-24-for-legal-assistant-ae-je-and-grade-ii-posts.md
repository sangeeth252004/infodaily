The Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) has officially announced the Combined Examination 2026, initiating the recruitment process for several key positions across departments under the Government of the National Capital Territory of Delhi. The notification, released recently, details openings for roles such as Legal Assistant, Assistant Engineer (AE), Junior Engineer (JE), and Grade II posts. Interested candidates will be able to submit their applications online starting from February 24.

This recruitment drive is expected to fill a substantial number of vacancies, offering opportunities for qualified individuals seeking government employment in Delhi. The DSSSB is the primary agency responsible for selecting candidates for various Group ‘B’ and Group ‘C’ posts for departments, local bodies, and autonomous organizations under the Government of NCT of Delhi. The Combined Examination 2026 represents a significant annual recruitment cycle for the board.

The application window for these posts will open on February 24, with specific closing dates and other critical timelines to be outlined in the detailed official advertisement. Aspiring candidates are advised to carefully review the complete notification on the official DSSSB website for comprehensive information regarding eligibility criteria, age limits, educational qualifications, selection procedures, and the application process.

The positions announced cater to diverse professional backgrounds and governmental needs:
*   **Legal Assistant:** This role typically involves providing legal support and administrative assistance within various government departments, requiring a background in law.
*   **Assistant Engineer (AE) & Junior Engineer (JE):** These engineering positions are crucial for infrastructure and development projects across Delhi. They often require degrees or diplomas in relevant engineering disciplines such as Civil, Electrical, or Mechanical engineering. The specific disciplines will be detailed in the comprehensive notification.
*   **Grade II Posts:** This category often encompasses a range of administrative or technical support roles, the exact nature of which will vary by department and be specified in the full recruitment advertisement.

The selection process for these DSSSB examinations typically involves a multi-stage approach. This generally includes a written examination, which may be a one-tier or two-tier system, followed by skill tests where applicable, and document verification. The specific scheme of examination, syllabus, and marking criteria will be published alongside the detailed notification.

Candidates are strongly encouraged to prepare well in advance and to monitor the official DSSSB portal for updates. The competitive nature of these examinations necessitates thorough preparation. The opening of applications on February 24 marks the formal commencement of a critical recruitment cycle for various Delhi government services, reinforcing the administration's ongoing efforts to strengthen its workforce and deliver public services effectively.

The detailed notification, containing specific post codes, the number of vacancies per post, pay scales, and all essential application guidelines, will be made available on the DSSSB website prior to the application commencement date. Candidates are urged to consult only the official channels for accurate information to ensure a smooth application process.
