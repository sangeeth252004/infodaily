The Gujarat Police Recruitment Board (GPRB) has recently released the provisional selection list and corresponding cut-off marks for candidates applying to 472 Unarmed Police Sub-Inspector (PSI) posts. This announcement represents a significant development in the ongoing recruitment process, making the details available for public access on the GPRB's official website.

Thousands of candidates who appeared for the PSI recruitment examinations have been awaiting this crucial update. The provisional list includes the roll numbers and names of candidates who have been deemed provisionally eligible based on their performance in the selection stages. Simultaneously, the GPRB has published the category-wise cut-off scores, providing transparency regarding the minimum marks required for provisional selection in different reservation categories.

This release is a standard procedure in large-scale government recruitment drives, serving as an interim step before the final merit list is compiled. It allows candidates to verify their status and understand the selection benchmarks. The Unarmed PSI positions are vital for bolstering law enforcement capabilities within the state of Gujarat, attracting a substantial number of applicants annually. The recruitment drive aims to fill critical vacancies and enhance the operational strength of the police force across various districts.

Key details regarding the announcement include:

*   **Total Posts:** The recruitment drive is for a total of 472 Unarmed Police Sub-Inspector (PSI) positions.
*   **Nature of List:** The released list is provisional, indicating that further stages, such as document verification, are pending before final selection.
*   **Cut-off Marks:** Category-wise cut-off marks have been published, detailing the minimum qualifying scores for provisional selection.
*   **Access:** Candidates can access the provisional list and cut-off details on the official website of the Gujarat Police Recruitment Board (GPRB).
*   **Purpose:** The provisional list allows candidates to check their current status and prepares them for subsequent stages of the selection process.

Following the release of the provisional list, the GPRB is expected to proceed with the next phase of the recruitment process, which typically involves the verification of documents for provisionally selected candidates. This stage is crucial for ensuring that all candidates meet the eligibility criteria and possess valid certifications as declared in their applications. Any discrepancies or failures to meet the required standards during document verification could lead to the disqualification of a candidate.

Upon successful completion of the document verification and any other stipulated procedures, the Gujarat Police Recruitment Board will issue a final merit list. This final list will confirm the candidates selected for appointment to the 472 Unarmed PSI posts. Candidates are advised to regularly monitor the official GPRB website for further updates and instructions regarding the next steps in this recruitment cycle.
