The Jammu and Kashmir Services Selection Board (JKSSB) has initiated a recruitment drive for 239 posts within the Health Department, pertaining to the 2026 recruitment cycle. Aspiring candidates are invited to apply online through the official JKSSB website, jkssb.nic.in, with the application window closing on March 25. This announcement signifies a significant opportunity for individuals seeking government employment in the region's healthcare sector.

The recruitment aims to bolster the human resource capacity of the Health Department across various cadres. Such initiatives are crucial for strengthening public health services and ensuring the availability of qualified personnel to meet the healthcare needs of the population in Jammu and Kashmir. The JKSSB, as the primary recruitment agency for various government departments in the Union Territory, plays a pivotal role in facilitating transparent and merit-based selections.

The application process is entirely online, designed to streamline candidate submissions and ensure accessibility. Candidates are advised to review the official notification thoroughly for detailed eligibility criteria, age limits, educational qualifications, and any specific requirements pertinent to the diverse roles advertised within the Health Department. The notification, available on the JKSSB portal, serves as the definitive source of information for applicants.

Key details regarding this recruitment drive include:

*   **Total Vacancies:** 239 posts
*   **Department:** Health Department
*   **Recruitment Cycle:** 2026
*   **Application Mode:** Online
*   **Application Deadline:** March 25
*   **Official Website for Applications:** jkssb.nic.in

Upon the closure of the application period, the JKSSB typically proceeds with a multi-stage selection process. This generally involves a written examination, followed by document verification for shortlisted candidates. The specifics of the examination pattern, syllabus, and marking scheme are usually outlined in the comprehensive recruitment advertisement or subsequent official communications from the Board. Candidates are encouraged to regularly check the JKSSB website for updates regarding examination dates, admit card availability, and further stages of the selection process. This recruitment drive is a key step towards addressing staffing requirements within Jammu and Kashmir's public health infrastructure.
