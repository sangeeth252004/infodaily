Authorities in Delhi have initiated a comprehensive investigation following the discovery of four deceased individuals, including a pregnant woman and her three daughters, in a residential area. The victims were found with their throats slit, indicating a violent crime. The woman's husband is currently missing, and police have launched a search operation to locate him as a critical component of the ongoing inquiry.

The tragic incident came to light recently, prompting a significant response from local law enforcement. The discovery of multiple fatalities, particularly involving a pregnant woman and children, has underscored the severity of the crime. Police officials have cordoned off the crime scene to preserve evidence and facilitate forensic examination.

Key details of the ongoing investigation include:
*   **Victims:** A pregnant woman and her three daughters. Their specific identities and ages have not been publicly disclosed by authorities as the investigation proceeds.
*   **Nature of Injuries:** All four victims sustained injuries consistent with their throats being slit, pointing to a brutal attack.
*   **Missing Individual:** The primary focus of the initial phase of the investigation is the whereabouts of the pregnant woman's husband. Police have not yet officially identified him as a suspect but are actively seeking him for questioning related to the incident.

Law enforcement agencies have established multiple teams tasked with locating the missing husband. These teams are utilizing various investigative techniques, including reviewing surveillance footage from the vicinity, gathering statements from neighbors and relatives, and tracing potential contacts of the missing individual. Forensic experts are meticulously examining the crime scene for any clues that could aid in understanding the sequence of events and identifying those responsible. The bodies of the victims have been sent for post-mortem examinations to ascertain the exact cause and time of death.

The motive behind the killings remains unknown. Investigators are expected to explore all possible angles, including domestic disputes, financial issues, or any other conflicts the family may have been involved in. The community has expressed shock and concern following the gruesome discovery, with residents awaiting updates from the police.

Authorities have urged anyone with information pertaining to the case or the whereabouts of the missing husband to come forward and assist with the investigation. The Delhi Police have affirmed their commitment to thoroughly investigate the matter and bring the perpetrators to justice, emphasizing the high priority assigned to this severe crime. The search for the missing husband is a crucial element in advancing the investigation towards a resolution.
