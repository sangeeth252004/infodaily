The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has officially released the examination schedule for the Lab Assistant recruitment drive set for 2026. Prospective candidates for these government positions will undergo a written test conducted over two days, specifically on May 9 and May 10, 2026. This announcement provides a clear timeline for thousands of applicants preparing for a career in laboratory services across various departments within Rajasthan.

This development follows the RSSB's standard procedure for major recruitment cycles, offering crucial clarity to candidates regarding the upcoming assessment phase. The examination aims to fill various Lab Assistant vacancies, roles critical for supporting scientific research, testing, and technical operations across numerous state government laboratories and institutions. The definitive dates allow candidates to structure their preparation effectively for what is anticipated to be a highly competitive selection process.

Key details released by the RSSB indicate the following:
*   **Examination Body:** Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB)
*   **Position:** Lab Assistant
*   **Examination Dates:** Friday, May 9, 2026, and Saturday, May 10, 2026
*   **Recruitment Cycle:** 2026
*   **Nature of Test:** Written examination

The RSSB is mandated to recruit eligible candidates for a wide range of non-gazetted posts in various state government departments, ensuring a merit-based selection process. The Lab Assistant examination is a significant component of its annual recruitment calendar, consistently drawing considerable interest from graduates and diploma holders in science streams. The typical selection process often involves a comprehensive written examination, followed by stages such as document verification and, in some cases, practical assessments. The exact structure for the 2026 examination will be detailed in the official information bulletin.
