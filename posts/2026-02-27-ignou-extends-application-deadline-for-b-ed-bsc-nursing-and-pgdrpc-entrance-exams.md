Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) has announced an extension to the application deadline for its Bachelor of Education (B.Ed), Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSc Nursing), and Post Graduate Diploma in Rural Psychiatric Nursing (PGDRPC) entrance examinations. Prospective candidates now have until January 3, 2023, to complete their registration online through the university's official website, ignou.ac.in.

This extension provides additional time for individuals aspiring to enroll in these specific programs for the upcoming academic session. The university's decision aims to ensure a wider pool of eligible candidates can apply, facilitating greater access to higher education for students across various regions. The entrance examinations are a mandatory step for admission into these competitive and specialized courses offered by IGNOU, a prominent distance learning institution in India.

The B.Ed program is designed for graduates interested in pursuing a teaching career, while the BSc Nursing program caters to in-service nurses seeking to enhance their professional qualifications. The PGDRPC course offers specialized training in psychiatric nursing within a rural context, addressing specific healthcare demands. These programs leverage IGNOU's flexible learning model, making them accessible to working professionals and students in diverse geographical locations.

Applicants are advised to prepare all necessary documents and review the eligibility criteria for their chosen program before initiating the online application process. The university has established a structured procedure for submission to ensure accurate and complete registrations.

**Key Details for Applicants:**

*   **New Application Deadline:** January 3, 2023
*   **Affected Programs:**
    *   Bachelor of Education (B.Ed)
    *   Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSc Nursing)
    *   Post Graduate Diploma in Rural Psychiatric Nursing (PGDRPC)
*   **Official Application Website:** ignou.ac.in
*   **Entrance Examination Schedule:** The entrance tests for these programs are scheduled to be conducted in January 2023.
*   **Admit Card Issuance:** Admit cards will be released for all candidates who successfully complete the registration process.

To register, candidates must visit the official IGNOU website, locate the dedicated link for the entrance examination application, and proceed with creating a new user account. The process involves filling out the comprehensive application form with personal and academic details, uploading scanned copies of required documents, and remitting the prescribed examination fee through the online payment gateway. Upon successful submission, applicants should download and retain a copy of their completed application form for future reference.

Following the conclusion of the extended registration period on January 3, 2023, IGNOU will commence the process of issuing admit cards to eligible candidates. These admit cards will contain vital information, including examination center details, specific dates, and timings for the entrance tests. Candidates are encouraged to monitor the official IGNOU website regularly for subsequent announcements regarding admit card release and the precise examination schedule throughout January 2023.
