The Footwear Design & Development Institute (FDDI) has officially announced the commencement of the registration process for its All India Selection Test (AIST) 2026. This examination serves as the primary gateway for aspiring students seeking admission to a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs offered by FDDI across its various campuses. The application window is now open, with prospective candidates able to complete their registration online via the institute's official website.

FDDI, established under the Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Government of India, holds the status of an 'Institution of National Importance' as per the FDDI Act, 2017. It is recognized for its specialized programs designed to cater to the demands of the footwear, leather goods, fashion, and retail industries. The AIST 2026 is a critical step for students aiming to pursue careers in these niche sectors, providing access to industry-aligned curricula and modern training facilities.

The institute offers a diverse portfolio of programs, including:
*   **Bachelor of Design (B.Des):** Specializations typically cover Footwear Design & Production, Fashion Design, Leather Goods & Accessories Design, and Retail & Fashion Merchandise. These four-year programs are structured to provide comprehensive knowledge and practical skills.
*   **Master of Design (M.Des):** Focused on advanced design principles and research within relevant fields.
*   **Master of Business Administration (MBA):** Specializing in areas such as Retail & Fashion Merchandise, equipping graduates with management expertise for the industry.

Eligibility criteria for these programs generally require candidates to have completed their 10+2 examinations or an equivalent qualification for undergraduate courses, and a bachelor's degree for postgraduate admissions. Specific academic percentages and subject combinations may be mandated, which are detailed in the official information brochure.

The All India Selection Test (AIST) 2026 is a computer-based examination designed to evaluate candidates' aptitude, general awareness, communication skills, and subject-specific knowledge pertinent to their chosen field of study. The test structure and syllabus are published by FDDI to guide applicants in their preparation. Successful candidates will proceed to a counseling process, where seats are allocated based on AIST scores and program availability.

Key details for applicants include:
*   **Application Mode:** Online registration through the official FDDI website.
*   **Application Period:** The registration window is currently open, with specific deadlines to be observed.
*   **Examination:** AIST 2026, a nationwide entrance test for admissions to all FDDI campuses.
*   **Programs Offered:** Bachelor of Design (B.Des), Master of Design (M.Des), and Master of Business Administration (MBA).
*   **Eligibility:** Varies by program, generally requiring 10+2 for UG and a bachelor's degree for PG courses.

Prospective students are strongly encouraged to review the comprehensive admission prospectus and information bulletin for AIST 2026, available on the FDDI website. This document contains precise details regarding eligibility requirements, application fees, examination dates, test centers, and the complete admission timeline. Timely submission of applications and adherence to all specified guidelines are crucial for consideration in the upcoming academic year. The admission cycle will proceed according to the announced schedule, culminating in orientation sessions for the new cohort.
