The Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test Cell (CET Cell) has announced an extension to the registration deadline for several professional undergraduate courses under the MHT CET 2026 cycle. Candidates aspiring for admission to 3-year LLB, 5-year LLB, Bachelor of Physical Education (BPEd), and integrated Bachelor of Education-Master of Education (BEd-MEd) programs now have additional time to complete their application process. The decision aims to accommodate more prospective students and was officially communicated by the CET Cell.

The MHT CET serves as a crucial entrance examination for admissions to various professional degree courses across Maharashtra. The extension specifically applies to the application windows for the academic year 2026-27, providing applicants with an opportunity to finalize their submissions who may have missed the initial deadlines. This adjustment ensures that all eligible candidates have a fair chance to apply for these competitive programs.

Applicants are advised to visit the official website of the Maharashtra CET Cell for the latest updates and to access the extended application forms. The revised schedule provides a specific timeframe for each affected program:

*   **3-Year LLB Program:** The new registration deadline for the three-year Bachelor of Laws course has been extended to June 10, 2025.
*   **5-Year LLB Program:** Registration for the integrated five-year Bachelor of Laws program will now close on June 10, 2025.
*   **Bachelor of Physical Education (BPEd):** Candidates for the BPEd course have until June 15, 2025, to complete their applications.
*   **Integrated BEd-MEd Program:** The application period for the integrated Bachelor of Education-Master of Education course has been extended to June 15, 2025.

The CET Cell's official portal will host detailed notifications regarding the extended timelines and any subsequent information pertaining to the examination schedule, admit card release, and result declarations. Candidates are strongly encouraged to verify all requirements and submit their applications well in advance of the new deadlines to avoid last-minute technical issues. This extension impacts a significant number of aspiring students across the state, offering them more flexibility in their academic pursuits for the 2026-27 academic year. Further announcements regarding the MHT CET 2026 examination dates and other procedural details are anticipated to follow the conclusion of the registration period.
