New Delhi – Prime Minister Narendra Modi extended his congratulations to the Indian men's cricket team on Saturday, June 29, 2024, following their triumph in the T20 World Cup 2024 final. India secured the championship by defeating South Africa by seven runs at the Kensington Oval in Bridgetown, Barbados, marking the nation's first T20 World Cup title since 2007.

Shortly after the match concluded, Prime Minister Modi shared a video message and a written statement on social media platforms, including X (formerly Twitter), expressing immense pride and joy. He stated, "This victory has filled every Indian heart with pride and joy," acknowledging the team's performance and its impact on the nation. The Prime Minister underscored the collective spirit and determination displayed by the players throughout the tournament, resonating with sentiments across the country.

The victory ended a 17-year wait for India to lift the T20 World Cup trophy, prompting widespread celebrations and an outpouring of support from fans and dignitaries alike. Prime Minister Modi specifically commended the team's resilience and strategic play in the high-stakes final.

Key aspects of the victory and the Prime Minister's recognition include:

*   **Historic Win:** India's second T20 World Cup title, following their inaugural win in 2007.
*   **Final Match Details:** India scored 176 for 7 wickets in 20 overs, with Virat Kohli contributing a crucial 76 runs. South Africa, in response, managed 169 for 8 wickets in their 20 overs.
*   **Key Performances:** Standout performances included Virat Kohli's match-winning half-century and Jasprit Bumrah's economical bowling spell, particularly in the critical final overs.
*   **Leadership:** Captain Rohit Sharma led the team through an undefeated campaign in the tournament, demonstrating consistent performance and tactical acumen.
*   **National Sentiment:** The Prime Minister's message reflected the broad national enthusiasm and the unifying power of sports in India. Celebrations erupted spontaneously across various cities immediately after the win.

The Prime Minister's office highlighted that the victory symbolises the hard work and dedication of the players, coaching staff, and the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI). This endorsement from the nation's leader serves to amplify the achievement and its significance within the broader national narrative.

Looking ahead, the victorious Indian squad is anticipated to return to India in the coming days, where further public felicitation events are likely to be organized. The Prime Minister's early and prominent congratulations set a celebratory tone for the team's return and the ongoing national festivities surrounding this sporting milestone.
