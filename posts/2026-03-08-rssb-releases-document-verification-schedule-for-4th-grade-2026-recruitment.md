The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has announced the document verification (DV) schedule for candidates shortlisted for the 4th Grade recruitment cycle 2026. The official notification, detailing specific dates, times, and venues for verification, has been made available on the board's official website, rssb.rajasthan.gov.in. This release marks a significant phase in the recruitment process, requiring all provisionally shortlisted applicants to present their original documents for verification.

This document verification stage is a mandatory step for candidates who have successfully cleared the initial examination phase for the 4th Grade positions. The process is designed to authenticate the educational qualifications, age, caste, and other eligibility criteria claimed by applicants during the application process. Candidates are advised to download the detailed schedule and their respective call letters from the RSSB portal to ensure they attend on their designated dates. Failure to appear for document verification as scheduled may result in the forfeiture of candidature.

The RSSB emphasizes the importance of meticulous preparation for this stage. Candidates must ensure all original documents, along with self-attested photocopies, are ready for inspection. The official notification outlines the complete list of required documents, which typically includes academic certificates, proof of identity, domicile certificates, caste certificates (if applicable), and copies of the initial application form. Any discrepancies found during the verification process could lead to disqualification.

Key details and instructions for shortlisted candidates include:
*   **Accessing the Schedule:** Candidates must visit rssb.rajasthan.gov.in and navigate to the "Latest News" or "Notifications" section to find the DV schedule and related instructions for 4th Grade recruitment 2026.
*   **Required Documents:** A comprehensive list is provided in the official notification. Commonly required documents include:
    *   Original educational mark sheets and certificates (from 10th grade onwards)
    *   Photo identity proof (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Voter ID, Passport)
    *   Domicile certificate for Rajasthan residents
    *   Caste/category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS) issued by the competent authority, if applicable
    *   Disability certificate (if applicable)
    *   Age relaxation proof (if applicable)
    *   Printout of the online application form and admit card
    *   Passport-sized photographs
*   **Reporting:** Candidates are instructed to report at the specified venue at least 30 minutes prior to their scheduled time to complete pre-verification formalities.
*   **Compliance:** Strict adherence to the instructions provided in the official notification is essential. Any candidate failing to present all required documents or whose documents are found to be inconsistent with their application may face rejection.

Following the successful completion of the document verification process, the RSSB will proceed with the final merit list preparation. This list will be compiled based on the candidates' performance in the written examination and the successful verification of their credentials. The final selections will lead to appointments for the 4th Grade positions within various departments across Rajasthan. Candidates are encouraged to regularly check the official RSSB website for further updates regarding the final results and subsequent joining formalities.
