Anna University, Chennai, has officially announced the commencement of online registration for the Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test (TANCET) and the Common Engineering Entrance Test for Admission - Post Graduate (CEETA-PG) 2026. Prospective candidates aiming for postgraduate admissions in affiliated colleges across Tamil Nadu can begin applying from October 15, 2025. The registration window will remain open until November 30, 2025, enabling applicants to complete the necessary steps via the official university website.

These entrance examinations are critical for admission to Master of Business Administration (MBA), Master of Computer Applications (MCA), Master of Engineering (M.E.), Master of Technology (M.Tech), Master of Architecture (M.Arch), and Master of Planning (M.Plan) degree programs for the academic year 2026-2027. Anna University is the designated authority responsible for conducting these state-level examinations on behalf of the Government of Tamil Nadu.

The TANCET exam facilitates admissions to MBA and MCA courses, while CEETA-PG serves as the gateway for M.E., M.Tech, M.Arch, and M.Plan programs. Thousands of students from Tamil Nadu and other states participate annually, seeking entry into various government, government-aided, and self-financing institutions affiliated with Anna University. The examinations assess candidates' aptitude and knowledge relevant to their chosen postgraduate disciplines.

**Key Details for Applicants:**

*   **Registration Period:** October 15, 2025, to November 30, 2025
*   **Official Website:** tancet.annauniv.edu
*   **Application Process:** All applications must be submitted online. No offline applications will be accepted.
*   **Application Fee:**
    *   ₹1000 for General Category candidates.
    *   ₹500 for Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribes (ST), and Persons with Disabilities (PWD) candidates.
    *   Fees are non-refundable and must be paid online through credit card, debit card, or net banking.
*   **Eligibility Criteria:**
    *   Candidates must hold a bachelor's degree of minimum three years duration in the relevant field.
    *   A minimum aggregate of 50% marks (45% for reserved categories) in the qualifying degree is generally required, though specific program requirements may vary.
    *   Indian nationality is required, with specific domicile rules for Tamil Nadu candidates.

Applicants are advised to meticulously review the official information brochure, which will be available on the university website from October 15, 2025. The brochure will detail the complete syllabus, examination pattern, eligibility criteria, and reservation policies. Accurate personal and academic details, along with scanned copies of photographs and signatures, are mandatory for successful registration.

Following the registration period, candidates can expect the release of admit cards in February 2026. The TANCET 2026 examination is provisionally scheduled for March 8, 2026, and CEETA-PG 2026 for March 9, 2026. The results are anticipated to be published in April 2026, followed by the counseling process for seat allocation. Candidates are strongly encouraged to regularly check the official website for updates and announcements regarding admit card release, examination centers, and subsequent counseling procedures.
