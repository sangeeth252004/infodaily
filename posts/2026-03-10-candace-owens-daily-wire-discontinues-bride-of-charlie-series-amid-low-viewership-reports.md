Candace Owens' media company, Daily Wire, has reportedly discontinued the Erika Kirk-created romance-drama series, 'Bride of Charlie'. The decision follows reports of consistently low viewership since the series' debut in late 2023. A representative for Daily Wire confirmed the discontinuation, attributing the move to the series not meeting internal performance benchmarks.

'Bride of Charlie' was launched as an original content offering within Daily Wire's expanding entertainment division. The series, written and directed by Erika Kirk, was designed to appeal to a conservative audience, presenting a narrative framework intended to resonate with specific cultural and social values. It premiered on Daily Wire's streaming platform as part of the company's broader strategy to provide alternative media content beyond its established political commentary and news programming.

Sources close to the production indicated that 'Bride of Charlie' struggled to attract and maintain a significant audience following its launch. Viewership figures reportedly remained consistently low, failing to meet the internal performance expectations established by Daily Wire leadership. While the company confirmed the decision to cease production, specific viewership numbers or financial details regarding the series' performance were not publicly disclosed. As of this report, neither Ms. Owens nor Ms. Kirk has issued a public statement directly addressing the series' end.

The discontinuation highlights the competitive landscape of the streaming industry and the challenges platforms face in cultivating an audience for original content, particularly within niche or ideologically focused markets. Daily Wire, co-founded by Ben Shapiro and Jeremy Boreing in 2015, has significantly invested in its entertainment division, producing feature films and series alongside its core news and opinion content. This expansion aims to diversify revenue streams and broaden subscriber engagement by offering a wider range of programming.

*   **Series Premise:** A romance-drama targeting a conservative demographic.
*   **Launch Date:** Late 2023.
*   **Reason for Discontinuation:** Reportedly low viewership and failure to meet internal performance benchmarks.
*   **Official Confirmation:** A Daily Wire representative confirmed the discontinuation.

Following the conclusion of 'Bride of Charlie', Erika Kirk is reportedly exploring new projects. Daily Wire, for its part, remains committed to its strategy of producing original content for its subscriber base. The company continues to develop and invest in a diverse slate of programming across various genres, aiming to serve its audience with a range of entertainment options. No immediate replacements for the specific content slot previously occupied by 'Bride of Charlie' have been announced by the platform.
