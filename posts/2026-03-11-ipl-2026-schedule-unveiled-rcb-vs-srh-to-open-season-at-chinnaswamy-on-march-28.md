This announcement, sourced from an official company statement, details the full fixture list for the upcoming T20 cricket league.

The release of the IPL 2026 schedule marks a significant milestone for cricket enthusiasts and stakeholders globally, providing clarity on the tournament's structure and key matchups. The IPL, an annual Twenty20 cricket league contested by ten city-based franchise teams in India, is recognized as one of the most prominent and financially lucrative professional sports leagues worldwide. Its return each year garners substantial viewership and economic activity.

The choice of M. Chinnaswamy Stadium in Bengaluru for the inaugural match continues a tradition of high-profile tournament openers at historic venues. The stadium serves as the home ground for Royal Challengers Bangalore, ensuring a familiar environment for the host team in their initial fixture. Royal Challengers Bangalore, a team with a considerable fan base, will face Sunrisers Hyderabad, former champions, setting the stage for a competitive start to the season.
