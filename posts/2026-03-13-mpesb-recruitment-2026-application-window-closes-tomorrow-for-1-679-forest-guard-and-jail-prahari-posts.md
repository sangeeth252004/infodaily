The Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB) will close the registration window tomorrow for its 2026 recruitment drive, which aims to fill 1,679 vacancies for the positions of Forest Guard and Jail Prahari. Prospective candidates are urged to complete their applications before the deadline, utilizing the direct application link provided by the board. This recruitment drive is a significant opportunity for individuals seeking public service roles within the state of Madhya Pradesh.

This recruitment initiative is part of the MPESB's ongoing efforts to strengthen essential state services, specifically in forest protection and correctional facility management. The roles of Forest Guard and Jail Prahari are critical for maintaining environmental security, managing state forests, and ensuring the safety and administration of penal institutions across Madhya Pradesh. Filling these positions is vital for the effective functioning of these departments and for upholding law and order within the state's jurisdiction. Government positions typically offer stability, competitive benefits, and opportunities for career progression, making these vacancies highly sought after.

The recruitment process targets a combined total of 1,679 positions. While specific breakdowns for each role were not detailed in the official announcement regarding the closing date, the combined figure represents a substantial intake for both the Forest Department and the Jail Department. The MPESB is the primary body responsible for conducting various competitive examinations for appointments to non-gazetted posts in the state government's departments. The current application phase signifies a crucial initial step for thousands of aspirants looking to secure a career in Madhya Pradesh's public sector.

*   **Total Vacancies:** 1,679 posts

*   **Recruiting Authority:** Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB)
*   **Application Deadline:** Closes tomorrow
