Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi, the organizing body for the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) 2026, is preparing for the imminent release of the examination's final answer key. Candidates who appeared for the national-level entrance exam in February 2026 are advised to monitor the official GATE portal, gate.iitd.ac.in, for its publication by late March 2026. This critical update precedes the official declaration of GATE 2026 results, which are provisionally scheduled for April 15, 2026.

GATE is a prominent national examination that assesses candidates' comprehensive understanding of various undergraduate subjects in engineering, technology, architecture, and science. A valid GATE score is a mandatory requirement for admission to postgraduate programs, including Master of Technology (M.Tech), Master of Science (M.S. by Research), and Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) courses, in centrally funded technical institutions across India. Furthermore, several Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) utilize GATE scores for their recruitment processes.

The GATE 2026 examination was conducted across four primary dates: February 1, 2, 8, and 9, 2026, encompassing multiple shifts and various examination centers nationwide. Following the successful conclusion of the examination cycle, IIT Delhi released the provisional answer key on February 25, 2026. A designated window for candidates to raise objections against the provisional answers, with supporting documentation, was provided from February 26 to March 1, 2026.

The forthcoming final answer key incorporates all valid challenges and corrections identified and accepted during the objection period. This updated key will serve as the definitive reference for the evaluation of candidates' responses and the accurate calculation of their raw scores. Its release is crucial for providing transparency in the evaluation process, enabling candidates to accurately estimate their performance before the official results are formally published.

To download the GATE 2026 final answer key once it is released, candidates should follow these steps:
*   Visit the official GATE 2026 website: gate.iitd.ac.in.
*   Locate and click on the "Final Answer Key" link, which will be prominently displayed on the homepage.
*   Select the specific paper code for which the answer key is required (e.g., ME for Mechanical Engineering, CS for Computer Science).
*   The answer key will be displayed as a downloadable PDF file.
*   Candidates are strongly advised to download and carefully review their respective answer keys.

Following the release of the final answer key, the GATE 2026 results are officially slated for declaration on April 15, 2026. Individual scores will be made accessible through the GATE Online Application Processing System (GOAPS) portal, where candidates can log in using their credentials. Successful candidates will subsequently participate in the GATE counselling process, with detailed schedules, participating institution information, and procedures to be announced by respective institutions and centralized counselling bodies in the weeks following the result declaration.
