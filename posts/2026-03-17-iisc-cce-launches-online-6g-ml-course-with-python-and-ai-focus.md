Bengaluru, India – The Centre for Continuing Education (CCE) at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) has recently unveiled a new online course focused on 6th Generation (6G) Mobile Communication and Machine Learning (ML). The program, designed to equip professionals with advanced skills in next-generation wireless technology, will incorporate modules on Python programming and Artificial Intelligence (AI), delivered through evening online classes.

This initiative by IISc CCE addresses the increasing demand for specialized expertise in the evolving telecommunications landscape. As the world transitions beyond 5G, the development of 6G networks is anticipated to bring unprecedented speeds, ultra-low latency, and pervasive intelligence, enabling new applications such as holographic communication, extended reality (XR), and truly autonomous systems. Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence are expected to be foundational to the design, optimization, and operation of these highly complex networks.

The course aims to provide participants with a comprehensive understanding of the core concepts underpinning 6G technology, alongside practical application skills in ML and AI relevant to wireless communications. Python, a widely used programming language in data science and AI, will serve as a key tool for hands-on learning and practical implementation of ML algorithms in a 6G context. This focus reflects the industry's shift towards intelligent, software-defined networks that leverage data analytics for enhanced performance and efficiency.

Key areas of focus within the program are expected to include:
*   **Foundations of 6G Technology:** Exploring the architectural frameworks, key enabling technologies, and research directions for next-generation wireless networks beyond 5G.
*   **Machine Learning for Wireless:** Application of various ML algorithms (e.g., supervised, unsupervised, reinforcement learning) for tasks such as resource allocation, channel estimation, anomaly detection, and predictive maintenance in communication networks.
*   **Artificial Intelligence in Telecom:** Understanding how AI will drive intelligent decision-making, network automation, and optimization across the 6G ecosystem.
*   **Python Programming for Data Science:** Practical sessions on using Python libraries and frameworks pertinent to data analysis, machine learning, and simulation in telecommunication environments.
*   **Network Optimization and Management:** Leveraging AI/ML techniques to enhance network performance, reduce energy consumption, and ensure robust connectivity in future wireless systems.

The course targets working professionals, engineers, researchers, and graduates with a background in telecommunications, electronics, computer science, or related fields who seek to upskill and remain competitive in the rapidly advancing tech sector. The evening online class format is intended to offer flexibility, allowing participants to balance their professional commitments with their educational pursuits. IISc, a prominent institution recognized for its scientific and engineering research, brings its academic rigor and expertise to this specialized program.

Enrollment details and specific module outlines are available through the official IISc CCE portal. This specialized course signifies a proactive step in preparing a skilled workforce for the challenges and opportunities presented by the upcoming era of 6G communication, contributing to India's technological advancement in global wireless innovation.
