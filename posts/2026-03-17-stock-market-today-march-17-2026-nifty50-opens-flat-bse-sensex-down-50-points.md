Indian equity benchmarks commenced trading on March 17, 2026, with the Nifty50 index opening flat, while the BSE Sensex recorded a marginal decline. The Nifty Midcap 100 and Nifty Smallcap 100 indices also traded with minor fluctuations, suggesting a lack of strong directional conviction across market capitalization segments.
