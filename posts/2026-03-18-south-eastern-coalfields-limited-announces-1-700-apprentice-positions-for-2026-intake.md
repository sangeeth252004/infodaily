South Eastern Coalfields Limited (SECL), a major subsidiary of Coal India Limited, has initiated a significant apprentice recruitment drive, announcing 1,700 vacancies for its 2026 intake. The opportunities are specifically targeted at graduates, diploma holders, and freshers seeking entry into various operational sectors within the company. This initiative aims to provide critical on-the-job training and skill development pathways for a substantial number of young professionals.

The recruitment program is designed to bring new talent into the coal sector, offering practical experience within a leading public sector undertaking. These apprenticeships are crucial for candidates looking to build foundational skills and gain exposure to industrial environments relevant to their academic qualifications. The positions are distributed across multiple departments and disciplines, reflecting SECL's diverse operational needs and its commitment to fostering a skilled workforce.

This large-scale recruitment drive underscores SECL's role in contributing to employment generation and national skill development goals. Apprentice programs at public sector companies like SECL are vital avenues for bridging the gap between academic learning and industrial application, providing participants with a structured training period often accompanied by a stipend. Such opportunities are particularly impactful for freshers, offering a defined entry point into professional careers without prior extensive experience.

Key details regarding the SECL Apprentice Recruitment 2026 include:

*   **Total Posts:** 1,700 apprentice positions
*   **Target Group:** Graduates, Diploma Holders, and Freshers
*   **Intake Year:** 2026
*   **Company:** South Eastern Coalfields Limited (SECL), a subsidiary of Coal India Limited
*   **Sectors:** Opportunities available across various operational sectors within SECL.
*   **Purpose:** Skill development, practical training, and career entry for young professionals.
