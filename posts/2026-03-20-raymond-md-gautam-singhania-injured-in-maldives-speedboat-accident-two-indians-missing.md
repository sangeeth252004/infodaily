Concurrently, two Indian nationals are reported missing in connection with the same incident. The information regarding the accident and its immediate aftermath was confirmed through an official announcement from Raymond Limited.

The incident has prompted immediate response efforts in the popular tourist destination. Reports indicate that Mr. Singhania received medical attention for his injuries. The nature and severity of his injuries have not been publicly detailed beyond the confirmation that he was injured. Raymond Limited's statement has focused on the well-being of its Managing Director and the collaborative efforts underway regarding the missing individuals.

Authorities in the Maldives have initiated search and rescue operations for the two Indian nationals who were aboard the speedboat at the time of the accident. These operations are critical and involve local emergency services and maritime units, focusing on the area where the incident took place. The identities of the missing individuals have not been released at this time.
