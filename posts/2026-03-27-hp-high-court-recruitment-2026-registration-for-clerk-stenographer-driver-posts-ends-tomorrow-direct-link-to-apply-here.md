The current recruitment cycle focuses on bolstering the administrative and operational capabilities of the High Court. Positions such as Clerks are crucial for managing court records, assisting with legal documentation, and providing general administrative support to judges and legal departments. Stenographers play a vital role in transcribing court proceedings, judgments, and legal communications, requiring precision and speed. Driver positions ensure the efficient and safe transportation of judicial officers and official documents, contributing to the smooth functioning of court operations. These roles are integral to the daily administration of justice and offer opportunities for public service within the state's judiciary.

Prospective applicants are advised to review the official notification thoroughly to ensure compliance with all prerequisites before attempting to apply.
