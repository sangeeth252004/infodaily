The Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) has officially released the admit cards for the LT Grade Teacher Examination 2026. Candidates who successfully applied for the recruitment drive can now download their respective hall tickets from the commission's official website, uppsc.up.nic.in. The admit card is a mandatory document for entry into the examination hall, containing critical information such as the candidate's details, exam venue, and schedule.

The release of the admit cards marks a crucial step in the recruitment process for Trained Graduate Teachers (TGTs) in various government inter colleges across Uttar Pradesh. The UPPSC aims to fill a significant number of teaching positions through this examination, which garnered widespread interest from eligible candidates across the state. This recruitment drive is projected to address the ongoing demand for qualified educators in secondary education within Uttar Pradesh.

The application window for the LT Grade Teacher positions opened on September 15, 2025, and concluded on October 15, 2025. The commission reported receiving over 1.5 million applications for approximately 10,768 posts across various subjects. These subjects include Hindi, English, Mathematics, Science, Social Science, Arts, Music, Computer, Biology, Sanskrit, Urdu, and Commerce. The vacancies are distributed across both male and female branches of government-aided secondary schools throughout the state.

The UPPSC LT Grade Teacher Examination 2026 is scheduled to be conducted on March 15 and March 16, 2026. The examination will be held in multiple shifts across a network of designated centers spanning all major districts of Uttar Pradesh. Candidates are advised to carefully check their admit cards for their specific exam dates, timings, and allotted examination centers to ensure they arrive prepared and on time.

Candidates can follow a structured process to download their admit cards:
*   **Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to uppsc.up.nic.in.
*   **Locate Admit Card Link:** On the homepage, click on the prominent link titled "Download Admit Card for LT Grade Teacher (Trained Graduate) Examination 2026."
*   **Enter Credentials:** A new login page will appear. Candidates must input their Registration Number, Date of Birth (in DD/MM/YYYY format), and the verification captcha code as displayed.
*   **Submit and Download:** After entering the details, click the "Submit" or "Download Admit Card" button.
*   **Print:** The admit card will be displayed on the screen. It is essential to download and print multiple copies for future reference and for presentation at the examination center.

The admit card contains vital information that candidates must verify for accuracy. This includes the candidate's name, roll number, registration number, photograph, signature, examination date, time, reporting time, examination venue address, and important instructions for the exam day. Any discrepancy found should be immediately reported to the UPPSC examination controller's office for rectification before the exam date.

Candidates are strongly advised to download their admit cards well in advance of the examination dates to avoid any last-minute technical difficulties. Along with a printed copy of the admit card, candidates are required to bring a valid government-issued photo identification proof (such as an Aadhaar card, PAN card, Voter ID, or Driving License) to the examination center. The UPPSC emphasized that no candidate will be allowed to appear for the examination without a valid admit card and photo ID. Following the written examination, the commission will proceed with the evaluation of answer sheets and the subsequent declaration of results, moving closer to the final selection and appointment of qualified teachers across the state.
