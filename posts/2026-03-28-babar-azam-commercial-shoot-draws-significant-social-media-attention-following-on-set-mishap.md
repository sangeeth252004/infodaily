Reports indicate that during the production of the advertisement, a specific sequence did not proceed entirely as planned. This raw footage, seemingly from the set, quickly gained traction, becoming a subject of public commentary.

This phrase trended as users offered their comedic interpretations of the event.

The incident underscores the immediate and far-reaching impact of social media on public figures, especially high-profile athletes engaged in commercial endorsements. Such campaigns are a significant component of modern sports marketing, connecting athletes with global brands and their consumer bases. Public scrutiny, encompassing both positive appreciation and critical or humorous reactions, often accompanies these campaigns, particularly when unforeseen events occur during their production or promotion.
