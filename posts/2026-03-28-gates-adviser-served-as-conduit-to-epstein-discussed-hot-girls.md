Emails from 2013 have revealed that Michael Auerbach, who served as an adviser to Bill Gates at the time, acted as a conduit to convicted sex offender Jeffrey Epstein, facilitating introductions to prominent individuals and engaging in discussions that included references to "hot girls." The communications detail Auerbach's efforts to expand Epstein's network among well-known figures.

The emails, dating from 2013, show Auerbach actively arranging meetings and connecting Epstein with influential people. Auerbach's role involved suggesting potential introductions to individuals such as the daughter of a former U.S. president, a former U.S. ambassador, a major university president, and a prominent media executive. One email from Auerbach to Epstein stated, "I'm going to New York on the 29th. I need to make some introductions for you."

Further correspondence between Auerbach and Epstein includes a particularly notable exchange concerning a meeting in Paris. In response to an email from Auerbach, Epstein wrote, "Hot girls always a plus!" Auerbach reportedly replied, "Yes I told you that. They will be there."

Michael Auerbach’s connection to Bill Gates stemmed from his involvement with Elevate Partners, a venture capital firm. He later became a senior adviser at Gates Ventures, the private office of Bill Gates. A spokesperson for Bill Gates reiterated Mr. Gates's regret over past interactions with Epstein, stating, "Bill Gates regrets any time spent with Epstein. Mr. Gates has no business or personal relationship with Auerbach." This statement clarifies the current relationship status between Gates and Auerbach.

Jeffrey Epstein, a financier, was a convicted sex offender who died by suicide in 2019 while awaiting trial on sex trafficking charges. The revelations about Auerbach's role add to the public understanding of the extensive network Epstein cultivated and the individuals who may have assisted him in connecting with influential circles.

A lawyer for Michael Auerbach stated that Auerbach "profoundly regrets having any association with Jeffrey Epstein." These newly disclosed emails continue to highlight the breadth of Epstein's connections and the efforts made by others to facilitate his access to powerful individuals. The ongoing scrutiny of Epstein's network continues to uncover details about his associations prior to his death.
