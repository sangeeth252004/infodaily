The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has recently issued the exam city intimation slips for the Sub Inspector (SI) Combined Competitive Examination 2026. This release provides candidates with advance information regarding their assigned exam district and shift, facilitating early logistical planning for the upcoming examination. Aspirants can access and download these slips from the RPSC's official website, rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in.

The intimation slip is a preliminary document, distinct from the final admit card, which contains specific details such as the roll number and the exact examination venue. Its primary purpose is to inform candidates about the broad geographical area where their examination will be conducted, along with the designated session (forenoon or afternoon). This proactive step by the RPSC aims to alleviate last-minute travel concerns for candidates appearing for the highly anticipated statewide competitive examination.

The Sub Inspector Combined Competitive Examination is a significant recruitment drive for various positions within the Rajasthan Police force, attracting thousands of applicants annually. The examination process typically involves multiple stages, beginning with a written test, followed by physical efficiency tests and interviews. Successful candidates are recruited to serve as Sub Inspectors, playing a crucial role in maintaining law and order across the state.

Candidates are advised to download their exam city intimation slips promptly to verify the details.

To download the RPSC Rajasthan SI exam city intimation slip, candidates should follow these steps:
*   Visit the official RPSC website: rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in.
*   Navigate to the "News and Events" section or locate the "Candidate Information" portal on the homepage.
*   Click on the link titled "Exam City Intimation Slip for SI Combined Competitive Exam 2026."
*   Enter the required login credentials, typically the application number and date of birth.
*   The exam city intimation slip will be displayed on the screen.
*   Review the details, download the PDF, and take a printout for future reference.

It is important for candidates to note that the exam city intimation slip does not serve as an entry pass to the examination hall. The final admit cards, containing essential details such as the candidate's roll number, exact examination centre address, and specific instructions, will be issued by the RPSC at a later date, typically a few days before the scheduled examination. Candidates are strongly advised to regularly monitor the official RPSC website for further announcements and updates regarding the release of the final admit cards and any other relevant information concerning the SI Combined Competitive Examination 2026.
