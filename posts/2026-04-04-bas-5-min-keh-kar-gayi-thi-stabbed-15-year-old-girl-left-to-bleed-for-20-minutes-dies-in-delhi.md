A 15-year-old girl succumbed to multiple stab wounds on Monday, May 27, 2024, a day after she was attacked in a narrow lane in the Seemapuri area of Northeast Delhi. The victim, identified as Muskan, daughter of Mohammad Shamim, was reportedly left bleeding for approximately 20 minutes before receiving medical attention.

Police officials confirmed that an FIR has been registered under Indian Penal Code (IPC) Section 302 (murder), and multiple teams have been formed to apprehend the accused, who has reportedly been identified. The incident occurred on Sunday evening, May 26.

According to police reports, a PCR call was received at 6:58 PM on Sunday regarding the stabbing. Police personnel arrived at the scene at 7:06 PM, finding that the victim had already been transported to Guru Teg Bahadur (GTB) Hospital by her family members. Muskan, who had sustained multiple stab injuries, died during treatment at the hospital the following day.

*   **Incident Timeline:**
    *   **Sunday, May 26, evening:** Muskan was stabbed in Seemapuri.
    *   **6:58 PM:** Police Control Room (PCR) received a call reporting the incident.
    *   **7:06 PM:** Police teams arrived at the scene.
    *   **Monday, May 27:** Muskan died at GTB Hospital during treatment.

Mohammad Shamim, Muskan's father, a daily wage earner, recounted that he had gone for Namaz when he received a call from a neighbor informing him about the attack. He described his daughter saying, "Bas 5 min keh kar gayi thi" (She had just gone out saying she would be back in 5 minutes). He returned home to find Muskan lying in a pool of blood.

Muskan's mother, Tabassum, stated she was home when she heard screams. Upon rushing out, she discovered her daughter bleeding profusely from several wounds. She informed investigators that she had previously seen the suspected accused near their house on one occasion.

Family members immediately attempted to staunch the bleeding. They initially transported Muskan on a two-wheeler before transferring her to a relative's car to reach GTB Hospital. A neighbor reported hearing a scream and witnessing Muskan collapse in the lane.

Authorities are actively searching for the identified accused. Investigations are ongoing to determine the full circumstances surrounding the attack and the motive behind it. Further details are expected as the police investigation progresses.
