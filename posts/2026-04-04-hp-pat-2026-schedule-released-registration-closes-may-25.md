The Himachal Pradesh Takniki Shiksha Board (HPTSB) has formally announced the comprehensive schedule for the Himachal Pradesh Polytechnic Admission Test (HP PAT) 2026. The official notification, published recently on the board's website, outlines critical dates for registration, examination, and subsequent admission processes. Prospective candidates aiming for diploma engineering and non-engineering programs in government and private polytechnic institutions across Himachal Pradesh are urged to note that the application window is set to close imminently.

Online registration for HP PAT 2026 will conclude tomorrow, Saturday, May 25, 2025, at 11:59 PM. This deadline marks the final opportunity for eligible candidates to submit their applications through the official HPTSB portal, hptsbedu.in. The board commenced the application process on April 10, 2025, providing a multi-week window for submission. Candidates are advised to complete their applications, including fee payment, before the specified cut-off time to avoid last-minute technical difficulties.

The HP PAT is a crucial state-level entrance examination facilitating admissions to various diploma courses. The 2026 examination is scheduled to be conducted on Sunday, June 15, 2026. This test evaluates candidates on their knowledge in subjects pertinent to their chosen fields, typically including Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and English, depending on the specific diploma program. Successful qualification in HP PAT is a prerequisite for securing seats in the participating polytechnic institutions.

Key details from the HP PAT 2026 schedule are as follows:

*   **Application Commencement:** April 10, 2025
*   **Application Deadline:** May 25, 2025 (11:59 PM)
*   **Admit Card Release:** June 1, 2026 (tentative)
*   **HP PAT 2026 Examination Date:** June 15, 2026 (Sunday)
*   **Provisional Answer Key Release:** June 18, 2026
*   **Window for Objections to Answer Key:** June 18-20, 2026
*   **Final Answer Key & Result Declaration:** July 5, 2026
*   **First Round of Online Counseling:** July 15-20, 2026

The application fee for HP PAT 2026 is INR 650 for General and OBC category candidates, while SC, ST, and PwD category candidates are required to pay INR 400. This fee must be paid online through specified payment gateways during the application process. Eligibility criteria generally mandate candidates to have passed the 10th class examination with a minimum aggregate of 35% marks, with Science and Mathematics as compulsory subjects. Specific course requirements may vary and are detailed in the official information brochure available on the HPTSB website.

Following the examination, the HPTSB will release a provisional answer key, allowing candidates a brief window to raise objections. A final answer key and the HP PAT 2026 results will be published thereafter, leading to the commencement of the online counseling process. This multi-stage counseling determines seat allocation based on merit rank, candidate preferences, and seat availability.

Aspiring candidates are strongly encouraged to review the complete information brochure available on the official website, hptsbedu.in, for detailed eligibility criteria, examination pattern, syllabus, and counseling procedures. Given the impending registration deadline, prompt action is advised for all interested applicants.
