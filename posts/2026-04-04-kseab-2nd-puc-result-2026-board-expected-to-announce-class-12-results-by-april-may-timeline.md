The Karnataka School Examination and Assessment Board (KSEAB) is expected to announce the results for the 2nd Pre-University Course (PUC) Class 12 examinations for the academic year 2026 by late April or early May. While a definitive date for the 2026 results has not yet been declared by the board, this timeline aligns with historical patterns for result dissemination. Students who will appear for the 2nd PUC examinations in 2026 are advised to monitor the official KSEAB websites, kseab.karnataka.gov.in and karresults.nic.in, for specific announcements.

The 2nd PUC examinations are a critical milestone for students in Karnataka, as these results determine eligibility for higher education courses across various universities and colleges. The examinations typically conclude in March, followed by a period of evaluation and data processing before the results are compiled and officially released. The KSEAB is responsible for conducting these examinations, evaluating answer scripts, and publishing the results in a timely manner each academic year.

In previous academic cycles, the KSEAB has consistently released 2nd PUC results within the April-May window. For instance, the 2024 results were declared on April 10, 2024, and the 2023 results were announced on April 21, 2023. This established trend provides a basis for anticipating the 2026 result timeline. The board typically issues a detailed notification several weeks in advance of the actual result declaration, outlining the precise date and time.

Upon release, students will be able to access their results online by entering their registration number and date of birth on the designated official portals. The online mark sheet will provide comprehensive details, including the student's name, roll number, subject-wise marks, total marks obtained, and the qualifying status.

To check the 2nd PUC results for 2026, students are typically required to follow a structured process:
*   Visit one of the official KSEAB websites: kseab.karnataka.gov.in or karresults.nic.in.
*   Locate and click on the "2nd PUC Result 2026" link, which will be activated on the day of the announcement.
*   Enter the assigned registration number and other required credentials in the specified fields.
*   Submit the details to view the provisional marks statement.
*   It is advisable to download and print a copy of the provisional result for immediate reference, though original mark sheets will be issued by educational institutions later.

Following the announcement of the main results, the KSEAB also provides provisions for re-evaluation of answer scripts and supplementary examinations for students who wish to improve their scores or those who did not qualify in the initial attempt. Specific dates and application procedures for these processes will be communicated by the board after the initial result declaration for 2026. Students and parents are advised to rely solely on official KSEAB channels for accurate and timely information regarding the 2nd PUC results.
