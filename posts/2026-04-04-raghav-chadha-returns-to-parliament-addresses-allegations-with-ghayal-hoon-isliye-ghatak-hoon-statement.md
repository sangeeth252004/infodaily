Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) Member of Parliament Raghav Chadha released a video statement on May 22, 2024, following the revocation of his suspension from the Rajya Sabha. In the message, widely circulated across social media, Chadha referenced the Hindi phrase "Ghayal hoon isliye ghatak hoon" (I am wounded, that is why I am dangerous) as he addressed the period of his suspension and the allegations that led to it.

Chadha's statement marked his public return to parliamentary duties after a nine-month suspension, which began on August 11, 2023. The suspension stemmed from allegations concerning the proposed Select Committee for the Delhi Services Bill. During the bill's consideration in the Rajya Sabha, four Members of Parliament — S. Phangnon Konyak, M. Thambidurai, Narhari Amin, and Sasmit Patra — alleged that their names were included in a proposal for the Select Committee by Chadha without their explicit consent. These complaints led to a motion for his suspension, which was subsequently approved, and the matter was referred to the Rajya Sabha's Privilege Committee for investigation.

On May 21, 2024, the Rajya Sabha Secretariat announced the revocation of Chadha's suspension, effective immediately. This decision followed the Privilege Committee's submission of its report on the matter. While the specifics of the committee's findings regarding the consent issue were part of the parliamentary record, the revocation paved the way for Chadha's re-entry into the parliamentary proceedings.

In his video message, Chadha described his suspension as a challenging period, attributing it to what he characterized as political targeting due to his vocal opposition and his party's stance on key legislative issues, particularly the Delhi Services Bill. His use of the "Ghayal hoon isliye ghatak hoon" quote, often invoked to signify resilience forged through adversity, conveyed a message of defiance and strengthened resolve. He conveyed that despite facing what he described as attempts to silence him, he remained committed to his political responsibilities and public service.

Chadha's return and his public statement underscore the ongoing political dynamics within the Indian Parliament. His re-entry ensures the AAP regains a prominent voice in the Rajya Sabha debates. As the political landscape continues to evolve, Chadha's re-engagement in parliamentary discourse is expected to draw attention, particularly on matters related to federalism and legislative oversight.
