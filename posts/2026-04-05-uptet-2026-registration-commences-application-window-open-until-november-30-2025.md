LUCKNOW, UTTAR PRADESH – The application process for the Uttar Pradesh Teacher Eligibility Test (UPTET) 2026 officially commenced on Monday, November 4, 2025. Aspiring teachers for government and government-aided schools across Uttar Pradesh can now register and submit their applications through the official website, updeled.gov.in. The Uttar Pradesh Basic Education Board (UPBEB), the conducting authority for UPTET, has set the deadline for online application submission as November 30, 2025. This annual examination serves as a mandatory requirement for individuals seeking primary and upper primary teaching positions in the state.

The UPTET holds significant importance for candidates aiming to enter the teaching profession within Uttar Pradesh's public education system. The examination is structured into two distinct papers: Paper I is designed for candidates aspiring to teach Classes 1 to 5, while Paper II targets those aiming for teaching positions in Classes 6 to 8. Applicants have the option to appear for one or both papers, contingent upon their specific educational qualifications and the desired level of teaching. Eligibility criteria typically mandate a graduation degree combined with a professional teaching qualification such as a D.El.Ed (Diploma in Elementary Education) or a B.Ed (Bachelor of Education). Comprehensive eligibility guidelines are detailed in the official notification published on the UPBEB portal.

A mandatory component of the UPTET 2026 application procedure is the One-Time Registration (OTR) system. This system requires all new applicants to complete an OTR process before proceeding to fill out the detailed application form. The OTR involves submitting essential personal information, verifying a registered mobile number and email address, and subsequently generating a unique registration ID. This ID is then used by candidates to log in, complete the comprehensive application form, upload required documents such, and facilitate the payment of the examination fee. For candidates who have previously completed OTR, guidelines regarding updating existing profiles or direct application, if applicable, are provided by UPBEB. A direct link to access the application portal is prominently displayed on the official website.

Key dates and details for the UPTET 2026 registration are outlined below:

*   **Online Registration Start Date:** November 4, 2025
*   **Last Date for Fee Payment:** November 28, 2025
*   **Last Date for Online Application Submission:** November 30, 2025
*   **Application Fee Structure:**
    *   General/OBC candidates: ₹600 for one paper, ₹1200 for both papers
    *   SC/ST candidates: ₹400 for one paper, ₹800 for both papers
    *   Persons with Disability (PwD): ₹100 for one paper, ₹200 for both papers
*   **Required Documents for Upload:** Scanned copies of a recent passport-sized photograph and candidate's signature, alongside relevant academic certificates.
*   **Anticipated Application Correction Window:** December 3-5, 2025

Following the successful completion of registration and fee payment, candidates will receive a confirmation. The admit cards for the UPTET 2026 examination are expected to be released in the weeks preceding the examination, which is tentatively scheduled for late January or early February 2026. Applicants are strongly advised to thoroughly review the official notification for complete details and to regularly check the UPBEB website for the most current updates concerning admit card issuance, examination dates, and subsequent result declarations. Qualification in UPTET is an essential prerequisite for participation in future teacher recruitment drives conducted by the state government.
