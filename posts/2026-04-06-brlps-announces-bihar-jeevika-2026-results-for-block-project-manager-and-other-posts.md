The Bihar Rural Livelihoods Promotion Society (BRLPS), widely recognized as Jeevika, has officially released the results for its Bihar Jeevika 2026 recruitment drive. The announcement covers various positions, notably including Block Project Manager and other allied posts. Candidates who participated in the selection process can now access and download their results directly from the official BRLPS website, brlps.in.

This result declaration marks a significant phase for thousands of applicants who sought to contribute to rural development initiatives across Bihar. The recruitment process typically involves a multi-stage selection, including written examinations and subsequent interview rounds, designed to identify qualified professionals capable of implementing the state’s poverty alleviation and livelihood promotion programs.

BRLPS operates under the aegis of the Rural Development Department, Government of Bihar, with a core mandate to empower rural households and enhance their socio-economic status. The Bihar Rural Livelihoods Project, commonly known as Jeevika, is a flagship initiative that focuses on mobilizing rural poor households into self-help groups (SHGs), strengthening their capacities, and facilitating access to sustainable livelihood opportunities. Positions such as Block Project Manager are crucial for the ground-level implementation and oversight of these extensive programs. These roles involve significant responsibility in community mobilization, project planning, resource management, and ensuring the effective delivery of development interventions at the block level.

The recruitment drive for Bihar Jeevika 2026 aims to bolster the existing workforce and expand the reach and efficiency of Jeevika’s operations throughout the state. The successful candidates will play an integral role in driving the project’s objectives, which include financial inclusion, agricultural development, skill enhancement, and market linkages for rural communities. The selection process is designed to ensure transparency and merit-based appointments, critical for public service roles.

To check their results, candidates are advised to follow specific instructions provided on the BRLPS website.

*   **Visit the official website:** Navigate to brlps.in.
*   **Locate the 'Results' section:** Look for a prominent link related to "Bihar Jeevika 2026 Results" or "Recruitment Results."
*   **Click on the relevant link:** Select the link corresponding to the Bihar Jeevika 2026 recruitment for Block Project Manager and other posts.
*   **Enter credentials:** Candidates may be required to enter their application number, roll number, date of birth, or other specific login details.
*   **Download and print:** The result will be displayed on the screen. Candidates are advised to download and print a copy for future reference.

Following the result announcement, successful candidates will typically proceed to subsequent stages, which may include document verification, medical examinations, and the issuance of offer letters. These steps are standard procedures to finalize appointments and ensure that all selected candidates meet the eligibility criteria and statutory requirements for government service. The onboarding of these new professionals is anticipated to further strengthen Jeevika's capacity to deliver on its mission of sustainable rural transformation in Bihar.
