On Tuesday, May 21, 2024, a significant security incident unfolded at the Delhi Assembly, raising concerns about the protection of the legislative premises. The event involved a car reportedly ramming an entrance gate, followed by unidentified masked individuals accessing the premises and placing flowers near the Speaker's office. This breach has prompted an immediate and thorough investigation by the Delhi Police and other security agencies.

According to preliminary reports, the incident occurred during the day when a speeding vehicle approached the main entrance of the Assembly complex. The car, whose make and registration details are part of the ongoing investigation, reportedly broke through at least one barricade before colliding with the main gate. Shortly after, masked individuals, whose exact number remains under investigation, were observed exiting the vehicle. These individuals proceeded further into the Assembly compound, specifically near the office of the Assembly Speaker, where they reportedly placed a bouquet of flowers. They subsequently departed from the scene.

The swift actions of security personnel on duty led to an immediate alert. Delhi Police was notified, and a team arrived promptly to secure the area and initiate an inquiry. A First Information Report (FIR) has been registered, and law enforcement agencies are analyzing available CCTV footage from within and around the Assembly complex to identify the vehicle and the individuals involved. The motive behind the incident remains a primary focus of the investigation. While the placement of flowers could be interpreted in various ways, authorities are treating the entire sequence of events as a serious security violation.

The incident underscores the persistent challenges in maintaining stringent security at critical government establishments. The Delhi Assembly, as a key legislative body, is typically under heightened security surveillance. Breaches of this nature prompt an immediate review of existing security protocols, including access control, perimeter defenses, and the deployment of security personnel. Political reactions have also emerged, with various leaders calling for a comprehensive inquiry and assurance of enhanced security measures to prevent future occurrences.

Key details identified in the ongoing investigation include:
*   **Date of Incident:** May 21, 2024.
*   **Location:** Delhi Assembly premises, specifically an entrance gate and near the Speaker's office.
*   **Actions:** A vehicle reportedly rammed a gate; masked individuals placed flowers.
*   **Response:** Delhi Police has registered an FIR and launched a full investigation, utilizing CCTV footage.
*   **Impact:** Raised concerns about security protocols at a legislative building.

The Delhi Police has assured the public that all resources are being deployed to apprehend those responsible for the security breach. The investigation is expected to delve into the identities of the masked individuals, the ownership of the vehicle, and any potential affiliations or motivations behind their actions. Meanwhile, security around the Delhi Assembly complex is anticipated to be intensified as a precautionary measure, with officials reviewing and implementing additional layers of protection. Further updates are expected as the investigation progresses and more facts become available.
