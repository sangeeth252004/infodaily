Puducherry, India – On Thursday, May 2, 2024, Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) National President J.P. Nadda unveiled a comprehensive list of election promises for Puducherry, detailing the party's agenda ahead of upcoming elections in the Union Territory. The announcement, made during a public address, outlined a series of pledges spanning economic relief, social welfare, youth empowerment, and infrastructure development, aiming to present a detailed vision for Puducherry's future.

Mr. Nadda emphasized that the manifesto reflects a commitment to the region's progress and the welfare of its citizens, aligning with Prime Minister Narendra Modi's broader vision for the nation. The BJP is currently in an alliance with the All India N.R. Congress (AINRC) in Puducherry, forming the incumbent government. The announced promises are positioned as a blueprint for governance should the alliance secure a renewed mandate.

The core of the BJP's electoral pledges addresses various demographics and sectors within Puducherry. Specific commitments include:

*   **Household and Economic Support:**
    *   Two free LPG cylinders annually for all eligible beneficiaries.
    *   Doubling of existing financial assistance provided to farmers.
    *   Increase in financial assistance for fishermen, from ₹5,000 to ₹10,000.
    *   Abolition of local body tax on commercial establishments for the first five years, aimed at boosting local businesses.
*   **Youth, Education, and Employment:**
    *   Provision of e-scooters for meritorious girl students.
    *   Financial aid for higher education initiatives.
    *   Creation of 2.5 lakh job opportunities for youth across Puducherry.
    *   Establishment of free WiFi zones across the Union Territory.
*   **Women and Social Welfare:**
    *   Maternity assistance of ₹25,000 for all expectant mothers.
    *   Free bus passes for women.
    *   Housing for all homeless individuals and slum dwellers within the next five years.
*   **Infrastructure and Development:**
    *   Development of Puducherry as an international tourist destination.
    *   Initiatives for the development of medical tourism and the establishment of a medical university.
    *   Ensuring water connectivity for every household.
    *   Focused development of industries, Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs), and startups.
    *   Smart city development projects, including underground drainage systems and LED lighting.
*   **Governance and Public Safety:**
    *   Enhanced focus on law and order, including the establishment of a special task force specifically to curb drug trafficking.
    *   Setting up a separate ministry dedicated to disaster management.

The unveiling of these promises marks a significant step in the BJP's campaign strategy for Puducherry, aiming to resonate with a broad spectrum of voters. The detailed manifesto will now be subject to public scrutiny as political parties intensify their outreach efforts in the lead-up to the impending assembly elections. Voters in Puducherry will evaluate these commitments as they prepare to cast their ballots, determining the next course of governance for the Union Territory.
