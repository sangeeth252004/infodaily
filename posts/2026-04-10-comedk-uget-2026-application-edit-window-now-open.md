Bengaluru, Karnataka – The Consortium of Medical, Engineering, and Dental Colleges of Karnataka (COMEDK) has activated the application correction window for its Undergraduate Entrance Test (UGET) 2026. This facility, crucial for candidates seeking admission to engineering courses in participating institutions, became accessible on May 15, 2026, and will remain open until May 20, 2026. Applicants who submitted their forms by the May 10, 2026 deadline are advised to review their entries and make necessary amendments via the official COMEDK website.

The edit window provides a designated period for candidates to rectify any errors or omissions in their previously submitted application forms. This mechanism is designed to ensure the accuracy of candidate data, which is paramount for the subsequent stages of the admission process, including admit card generation, examination, result compilation, and counselling. Incorrect information could lead to discrepancies or challenges during document verification.

Accessing the correction window requires candidates to log in to their applicant portal using their registered credentials, including application number and password. Upon successful login, the application form will be presented with specific fields marked as editable. COMEDK officials emphasize that candidates should meticulously review all details before finalizing and saving any changes. Once the corrected form is submitted and saved, further modifications will not be permitted after the closing date of the edit window.

Key editable fields typically include, but are not limited to, the following:

*   **Personal Information:** Minor corrections to the candidate's name, parent's name, date of birth, and gender. Significant changes to primary identifiers like the full name might require specific documentary proof or may be restricted.
*   **Contact Details:** Updates to email addresses (though the primary registered email often remains unchangeable), mobile numbers, and correspondence addresses.
*   **Academic Qualifications:** Rectification of marks, percentages, or board details for qualifying examinations.
*   **Category Details:** Changes to reservation category (e.g., General, SC, ST, OBC), provided supporting documents are available and meet criteria.
*   **Photograph and Signature:** Re-uploading of scanned images if the previously submitted ones were unclear, incorrect, or did not meet specified dimensions and file size.
*   **Examination Centre Preference:** Modifying the choice of test city or centre, subject to availability.

It is important for applicants to note that certain core details, such as the unique application number and, in many cases, the primary registered email ID and mobile number linked to the initial registration, are usually non-editable to maintain the integrity of the application process. Candidates are also reminded that any changes made must be accurate and verifiable.

Following the closure of the edit window on May 20, 2026, COMEDK is expected to release the admit cards for UGET 2026. The examination is tentatively scheduled for early June 2026. Candidates are strongly advised to download and print a copy of their updated application form for future reference and to stay updated with announcements on the official COMEDK website regarding admit card release, examination day guidelines, and subsequent admission procedures.
