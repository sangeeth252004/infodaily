Patna, Bihar – The application window for Class 11th admissions for the academic year 2026 in Bihar is scheduled to close on April 18. Students aspiring to enroll in Intermediate (10+2) courses across various colleges and schools affiliated with the Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) must submit their applications through the Online Facilitation System for Students (OFSS) portal before the stipulated deadline.

The OFSS portal serves as the centralized online platform for admission to Class 11th (Intermediate) in all 10+2 level educational institutions within Bihar. This system streamlines the application process, allowing eligible candidates to apply for admission into Arts, Science, and Commerce streams. The initiative aims to provide a transparent and accessible admission mechanism for thousands of students completing their matriculation (Class 10th) examinations.

Eligibility for these admissions primarily requires candidates to have successfully passed the Matriculation (Class 10th) examination from the Bihar School Examination Board or any other recognized national or state board. Applicants are advised to review the detailed eligibility criteria available on the official OFSS website to ensure compliance before proceeding with their applications. The application process typically involves online registration, filling out personal and academic details, uploading necessary documents, and payment of the application fee.

Key details for prospective applicants include:

*   **Application Deadline:** April 18, 2024
*   **Application Platform:** OFSS Bihar official portal (ofssbihar.in)
*   **Target Academic Year:** 2026
*   **Eligibility:** Passing the Matriculation (Class 10th) examination from BSEB or a recognized board.
*   **Required Documents (typical):** Matric mark sheet, admit card, passport-sized photographs, caste certificate (if applicable), and other identification proofs.
*   **Application Fee:** A non-refundable fee is required to complete the application process.

Upon the conclusion of the application period, the BSEB, through the OFSS portal, will proceed with the preparation and release of merit lists. These lists will be generated based on the marks obtained by students in their Class 10th examination and the preferences indicated during the application process. Subsequent rounds of seat allocation and the physical admission process at the designated institutions will follow. Students are advised to monitor the official OFSS website for updates regarding merit lists, admission schedules, and further instructions to ensure timely completion of their enrollment.
