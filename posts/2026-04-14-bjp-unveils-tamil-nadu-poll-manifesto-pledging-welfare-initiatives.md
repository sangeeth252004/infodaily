The Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) on Monday, April 22, 2024, unveiled its election manifesto for the upcoming polls in Tamil Nadu, presenting a range of welfare and development proposals aimed at various segments of the state's population. The manifesto, released by senior party leaders in Chennai, outlined key commitments including free LPG cylinders for eligible households and a direct cash transfer of Rs 2,000 for women beneficiaries.

The document emphasizes a vision for economic growth coupled with social welfare, seeking to address critical areas such as household expenses, women's empowerment, infrastructure development, and agricultural support. The party's state leadership affirmed that the proposals were formulated following extensive consultations with local communities and stakeholders across Tamil Nadu.

Key promises detailed in the BJP's Tamil Nadu election manifesto include:

*   **Free LPG Cylinders:** The manifesto proposes the provision of three free Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) cylinders annually to families identified as economically vulnerable, aiming to alleviate the burden of rising fuel costs.
*   **Direct Cash Transfer for Women:** A recurring direct cash transfer of Rs 2,000 is pledged for women heads of households, intended to provide financial assistance and enhance their economic stability.
*   **Agricultural Support:** Commitments include increasing Minimum Support Price (MSP) for key crops grown in Tamil Nadu, improving irrigation infrastructure, and offering subsidies for modern farming techniques to boost agricultural productivity and farmer incomes.
*   **Infrastructure Development:** The party has promised accelerated investment in critical infrastructure projects, including the expansion of national and state highways, improved digital connectivity, and the establishment of new industrial corridors to foster job creation.
*   **Education and Skill Development:** Proposals include upgrading existing educational institutions, establishing new vocational training centers, and launching skill development programs tailored to industry demands, particularly targeting the youth.
*   **Healthcare Enhancements:** The manifesto outlines plans to strengthen public health infrastructure, increase the number of primary health centers, and expand access to affordable healthcare services for all citizens.
*   **Fisheries Sector Support:** Specific initiatives for the fishing community involve modernizing fishing harbors, providing financial assistance for deep-sea fishing equipment, and ensuring welfare schemes for fishermen.

The release of the manifesto marks a significant phase in the party's campaign strategy for the state, which is preparing for general elections. Party leaders indicated that the implementation of these promises would aim to significantly improve living standards and foster comprehensive development across Tamil Nadu. The specific modalities and eligibility criteria for these schemes are expected to be detailed further as the election campaign progresses.
