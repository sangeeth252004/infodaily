HYDERABAD, Telangana – The Telangana State Board of Intermediate Education (TSBIE) has announced an extension for the fee payment deadline for the Intermediate Public Advanced Supplementary Examination (IPASE) 2026. Students who intend to appear for the supplementary examinations now have additional time to complete their registration and fee submission. The new deadline has been set for May 20, 2026, providing a crucial window for eligible candidates.

Previously, the last date for fee payment was May 10, 2026. This extension aims to accommodate a larger number of students, ensuring that all eligible candidates have ample opportunity to apply for the IPASE 2026 without undue pressure. The supplementary examinations are critical for students who did not pass all subjects in the recently concluded Intermediate Public Examinations (IPE) March 2026, or for those who wish to improve their scores in specific subjects. Successful completion of these examinations is essential for academic progression and eligibility for higher education.

The IPASE 2026 serves as a second chance for intermediate students to qualify for their respective streams, whether in Science, Commerce, or Arts. The examinations are typically held a few weeks after the announcement of the main examination results, allowing sufficient time for students to prepare and register. The TSBIE facilitates the fee payment process primarily through its official website, where students can access the application portal using their hall ticket numbers and other required details.

Officials from the TSBIE emphasized the importance of adhering to the new deadline. The Board has urged colleges to guide their students through the payment process and ensure that all applications are submitted accurately and on time. Delays beyond the extended deadline will not be accommodated, as the Board needs to finalize arrangements for hall ticket generation and examination centre allocations. This structured approach is vital for the smooth conduct of examinations across the state.

Key details regarding the TS Inter Supplementary Exam 2026 fee payment are as follows:
*   **Original Fee Payment Deadline:** May 10, 2026
*   **Extended Fee Payment Deadline:** May 20, 2026
*   **Eligibility:** Candidates who failed one or more subjects in the Intermediate Public Examinations, March 2026, or those aiming to improve their scores.
*   **Application Mode:** Online, via the official TSBIE portal.
*   **Official Website:** Students should visit tsbie.cgg.gov.in for the payment link and detailed instructions.
*   **Required Information:** Hall ticket number of the IPE March 2026, date of birth, and relevant subject details.
*   **Expected Examination Period:** The IPASE 2026 examinations are provisionally expected to be conducted in the first two weeks of June 2026, with a detailed timetable to be released subsequently by the TSBIE.

Students and their parents are advised to promptly complete the fee payment process before the new deadline of May 20, 2026. Utilizing this extended period will prevent last-minute complications and ensure eligibility for the upcoming supplementary examinations. Further announcements regarding the examination schedule and hall ticket availability will be published on the TSBIE's official website.
