The Joint Admission Board (JAB) has announced that registration for the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Advanced 2026, which facilitates admissions to the prestigious Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) for the academic year 2026-27, is scheduled to commence tomorrow, June 1, 2024. Aspiring engineering students can access the application portal on the official JEE Advanced website, jeeadv.ac.in, marking an early start to the admissions cycle for one of India's most competitive entrance examinations.

JEE Advanced serves as the exclusive pathway for admissions to undergraduate engineering programs across the 23 IITs, globally recognized for their academic rigor and research contributions. Candidates must first qualify in JEE Main to be eligible to appear for JEE Advanced. The comprehensive examination evaluates candidates' in-depth understanding and analytical capabilities in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. The detailed information brochure, including specific dates for registration closure, admit card release, and examination dates, is expected to be released by the JAB alongside the opening of the application portal.

**Key Details for Prospective Applicants:**

To successfully navigate the application process, candidates must adhere to specific eligibility requirements, understand the examination structure, and follow the prescribed application steps.

*   **Eligibility Criteria:**
    *   Candidates must have qualified JEE Main 2026 and be among the top 2,50,000 successful candidates (including all categories) as per the JAB's percentile cut-off.
    *   The candidate's date of birth must fall on or after October 1, 2001, for General category candidates. Age relaxation provisions apply for Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), and Persons with Disabilities (PwD) categories as per official norms.
    *   A candidate is permitted a maximum of two attempts at JEE Advanced in consecutive years.
    *   Applicants must have passed their Class 12 (or equivalent) examination in either 2025 or 2026. Candidates who appeared and passed Class 12 in 2024 are also eligible if they meet other stipulated conditions.
    *   A minimum of 75% aggregate marks in the Class 12 (or equivalent) examination is required, or candidates must be in the top 20 percentile of their respective qualifying board examination. For SC/ST/PwD candidates, the minimum aggregate percentage is 65%.

*   **Exam Pattern:**
    *   JEE Advanced typically consists of two compulsory papers: Paper 1 and Paper 2, each with a duration of three hours.
    *   Each paper is divided into three sections: Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
    *   Question types generally include Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) with single or multiple correct options, numerical answer type questions, and potentially comprehension-based questions.
    *   Negative marking is usually applicable for incorrect answers in specific question formats, emphasizing accuracy and strategic answering.
    *   The examination is conducted exclusively in computer-based test (CBT) mode across designated centers.

*   **Steps to Apply:**
    *   **Step 1: Visit the Official Website:** Navigate to jeeadv.ac.in, the dedicated portal for JEE Advanced 2026.
    *   **Step 2: Access Registration Link:** Click on the prominently displayed 'Registration Link' for JEE Advanced 2026.
    *   **Step 3: Login:** Use your JEE Main 2026 application number and password to log in to the portal.
    *   **Step 4: Fill Application Form:** Accurately provide personal details, academic qualifications, contact information, and other required data.
    *   **Step 5: Upload Documents:** Upload scanned copies of photographs, signatures, Class 10 and 12 mark sheets, category certificates (if applicable), and other necessary documents as per the specified format and size guidelines.
    *   **Step 6: Pay Application Fee:** Complete the payment of the application fee online using net banking, credit card, or debit card. Fees vary by category as per the official notification.
    *   **Step 7: Review and Submit:** Thoroughly review all entered information and uploaded documents before final submission to avoid discrepancies.
    *   **Step 8: Print Confirmation Page:** Download and print the confirmation page for future reference and record-keeping.

Following the registration period, candidates will be able to download their admit cards. The JAB will subsequently announce the precise examination dates for JEE Advanced 2026 and the schedule for result declaration, guiding aspirants through the critical next stages of the admission cycle.
