Rajasthan Royals (RR) secured a decisive 40-run victory over Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) in an Indian Premier League (IPL) 2026 fixture held on May 10, 2026, at the Dr. DY Patil Sports Academy in Navi Mumbai. The comprehensive win propelled RR further up the league standings, while the defeat presented a significant setback for LSG, intensifying the competitive pressure across the tournament.

Batting first after winning the toss, Rajasthan Royals posted a formidable total of 198 for 5 wickets in their allotted 20 overs. Opener Jos Buttler anchored the innings with a blistering 78 runs off 45 deliveries, featuring five boundaries and six maximums. Captain Sanju Samson contributed a quickfire 45 runs from 28 balls, building a crucial partnership with Buttler. LSG's Ravi Bishnoi was the pick of the bowlers, claiming 2 wickets for 32 runs, but the Royals' aggressive batting ensured a challenging target.

In response, Lucknow Super Giants struggled to build momentum, ultimately falling short as they were restricted to 158 for 9 wickets. Despite a resilient effort from Nicholas Pooran, who scored 52 runs off 30 balls, LSG batsmen failed to establish sustained partnerships against a disciplined RR bowling attack. The early loss of key wickets, including captain KL Rahul for a modest score, severely hampered their chase. RR’s spinner Yuzvendra Chahal led the bowling effort, taking 3 wickets for 25 runs, effectively stifling LSG’s scoring rate in the middle overs.

The implications of this result are significant for both franchises. For Rajasthan Royals, the victory marks their fifth win of the season, bolstering their net run rate and strengthening their contention for a playoff spot. The performance showcased a balanced team effort in both batting and bowling departments. Conversely, the 40-run defeat for Lucknow Super Giants represents a critical blow to their campaign, pushing them further down the league table and requiring a strong resurgence in their upcoming matches to remain in contention.
