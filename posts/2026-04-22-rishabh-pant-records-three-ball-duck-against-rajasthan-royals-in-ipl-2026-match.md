Rishabh Pant, captain and wicketkeeper-batsman for the Delhi Capitals, was dismissed for a three-ball duck during his team's Indian Premier League (IPL) 2026 match against the Rajasthan Royals on April 22, 2026, at the Sawai Mansingh Stadium in Jaipur. This performance continues a challenging period for the prominent left-handed batsman, raising questions about his current batting form in the ongoing tournament.

The incident occurred in the seventh over of the Delhi Capitals' innings, after they had lost two early wickets. Pant, arriving at the crease under pressure, faced Rajasthan Royals' fast bowler Trent Boult. On his third delivery, a full-length inswinger, Pant was trapped lbw, leading to the umpire’s immediate decision. His dismissal left the Delhi Capitals struggling at 48 for 3 wickets, further compounding the team's batting woes in a crucial fixture.

*   **Score at Dismissal:** Delhi Capitals 48/3
