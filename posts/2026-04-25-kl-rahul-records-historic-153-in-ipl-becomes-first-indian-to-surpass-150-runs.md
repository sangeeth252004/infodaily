Lucknow, India – On May 5, 2026, batsman KL Rahul etched his name into the annals of Indian Premier League (IPL) history, becoming the first Indian player to register a score of 150 runs or more in a single innings. Playing for the Lucknow Super Giants (LSG) against Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) at the Bharat Ratna Shri Atal Bihari Vajpayee Ekana Cricket Stadium in Lucknow, Rahul scored an unbeaten 153 runs, propelling his team to a commanding total.

The monumental innings by the LSG captain marks a significant milestone in the league's nineteen-year history, setting a new benchmark for Indian batsmen. Prior to this performance, the exclusive club of players with scores exceeding 150 runs in the IPL comprised only overseas cricketers, including Chris Gayle (175*), Brendon McCullum (158*), and AB de Villiers (133*, although not 150+, it was a significant high score often referenced), highlighting the rarity and magnitude of Rahul's achievement. This individual performance not only underscored his batting prowess but also provided a substantial foundation for LSG's match total of 230 for 3 wickets.

Rahul's innings was characterized by aggressive yet controlled stroke play. He faced 65 deliveries, dispatching the ball to the boundary 12 times and clearing it for 10 sixes, achieving a strike rate exceeding 235. His significant contribution helped stabilize the innings after an early wicket and accelerated the scoring rate in the middle and death overs. This performance is a testament to his consistent form in recent IPL seasons, where he has frequently been among the leading run-scorers.

Key highlights of the innings include:
*   **Record-Breaking Performance:** First Indian to achieve a score of 150+ in IPL history.
*   **Individual Score:** 153 runs not out from 65 balls.
*   **Boundary Count:** 12 fours and 10 sixes.
*   **Match Context:** Played against Royal Challengers Bangalore at the Ekana Cricket Stadium.
*   **Team Contribution:** Pushed Lucknow Super Giants to a total of 230/3.

The achievement places Rahul in an elite category of IPL batsmen and will likely be discussed as one of the standout performances of the IPL 2026 season. Cricket analysts have noted the significance of an Indian batsman breaking this particular scoring barrier, indicating the evolving landscape of T20 cricket in the country. This milestone also solidifies Rahul's reputation as one of the most impactful batsmen in the tournament.

Following this record-setting innings, KL Rahul is expected to continue leading the Lucknow Super Giants in their upcoming fixtures, with increased attention on his ability to maintain this high level of performance. The focus now shifts to how this individual brilliance might influence LSG's campaign in the current season and potentially impact Rahul's standing in future national team selections.
