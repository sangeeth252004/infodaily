Rabin Ghosh, a 50-year-old Hindu temple caretaker, was found deceased, hanging from a tree, near a Kali temple in Kalmakanda upazila, Netrokona district, Bangladesh, on the morning of Saturday, June 1, 2024. Local authorities have initiated an investigation into the circumstances surrounding his death, as conflicting accounts have emerged regarding its cause.

The discovery prompted an immediate response from law enforcement. Kalmakanda Police Station Officer-in-Charge (OC) Shaukat Akhand confirmed the recovery of Ghosh's body. OC Akhand stated that preliminary observations suggested the possibility of death by suicide. Following standard procedure, the body was transported to the Netrokona Sadar Hospital morgue for a comprehensive autopsy, a critical step to ascertain the precise cause of death and gather forensic evidence.

However, family members and relatives of Rabin Ghosh have vehemently rejected the police's initial assessment. They contend that Ghosh was murdered and have called for a thorough investigation to identify and apprehend those responsible. Relatives informed reporters that Ghosh had recently received threats, though specific details of these threats were not disclosed. They also emphasized that Ghosh had no known financial difficulties, personal disputes, or family issues that would lead him to take his own life, further reinforcing their belief that foul play was involved.

The incident has brought attention to the safety of religious figures in the region. The Kali temple, where Ghosh served as caretaker, is a local religious site, and his sudden death under contested circumstances has raised concerns among the community. Police officials have confirmed that all aspects of the case are being carefully examined. Investigators are gathering evidence, interviewing witnesses, and considering all available information to piece together the events that led to Ghosh's death.

As of the latest reports, the investigation remains ongoing. Authorities are awaiting the detailed autopsy report from Netrokona Sadar Hospital, which is expected to provide crucial insights into the cause and manner of death. No definitive conclusion regarding the circumstances of Rabin Ghosh's death has been released, and no arrests have been made. The police have not yet confirmed whether they are investigating the case as a suicide, murder, or an unexplained death, pending further findings.
