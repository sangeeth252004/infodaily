The Chhattisgarh Board of Secondary Education (CGBSE) is scheduled to announce the results for its Class 10 (High School Certificate Examination) and Class 12 (Higher Secondary School Certificate Examination) 2026 tomorrow, Friday, May 9, 2026. The announcement will be made via a press conference at the CGBSE headquarters in Raipur, with results subsequently available for students to access online. This declaration will finalize the academic performance for hundreds of thousands of students across the state who appeared for the board examinations earlier this year.

Students will be able to check and download their provisional mark sheets from the official CGBSE websites, cgbse.nic.in and results.cg.nic.in, starting at approximately 12:30 PM Indian Standard Time (IST). The release marks a critical juncture for students, determining their eligibility for higher education and future career paths. These examinations, conducted annually, represent a key educational milestone in the academic calendar of Chhattisgarh.

The CGBSE conducted the Class 10 board examinations from March 1 to March 23, 2026, while the Class 12 examinations took place from March 1 to March 23, 2026. Approximately 3.5 lakh students appeared for the Class 10 examination, and around 3 lakh students took the Class 12 examination this year. The evaluation process involved thousands of educators across various centers, culminating in the compilation of these results. The board aims to ensure transparency and accuracy in the result declaration process.

To access their results, students will need their respective roll numbers. The online result facility is designed to provide immediate access to individual scores and overall performance details. After checking their results online, students are advised to download and print their provisional mark sheets for immediate reference. Original mark sheets will be distributed by respective schools at a later date, typically a few weeks after the online declaration.

**Key Details for Checking CGBSE Results 2026:**

*   **Date of Announcement:** Friday, May 9, 2026
*   **Time of Announcement:** Approximately 12:30 PM IST
*   **Official Websites:**
    *   cgbse.nic.in
    *   results.cg.nic.in
*   **Information Required:** Student's Roll Number

**Steps to Download CGBSE Class 10, 12 Results 2026:**

1.  Visit one of the official CGBSE websites: cgbse.nic.in or results.cg.nic.in.
2.  Locate and click on the link for "High School Result 2026" or "Higher Secondary Result 2026" on the homepage.
3.  Enter your examination roll number in the designated field.
4.  Click on the "Submit" or "Get Result" button.
5.  Your provisional mark sheet will be displayed on the screen.
6.  Download and print a copy for future reference.

In the previous academic year, 2025, the overall pass percentage for CGBSE Class 10 was approximately 75.60%, while for Class 12, it stood at about 80.20%. These figures provide a contextual benchmark for the 2026 results. The CGBSE maintains a rigorous examination and evaluation framework to uphold academic standards within the state, impacting career trajectories for a significant portion of the youth population.

Following the declaration of results, students will have the option to apply for re-evaluation or re-totaling of their answer sheets, should they believe there has been a discrepancy in their marks. The application window and procedures for these processes, along with supplementary examinations for students who do not pass in one or more subjects, will be announced by the CGBSE in due course. The board aims to facilitate all necessary post-result processes efficiently to support students in their academic journey.
