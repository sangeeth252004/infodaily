Bengaluru, India – Infosys, a global leader in next-generation digital services and consulting, announced on Monday, May 13, 2024, the appointment of Nitin Paranjpe as the Vice Chairman of its Board of Directors. This significant leadership development was accompanied by a clear statement from Chairman Nandan Nilekani, who affirmed that there are currently no immediate plans for a transition in the chairman's role, underscoring continuity at the helm of the company.

Mr. Paranjpe’s elevation to Vice Chairman is effective immediately. He originally joined the Infosys Board as an independent director in January 2024, bringing with him a wealth of experience from the global business landscape. Prior to his tenure at Infosys, Mr. Paranjpe held several prominent executive positions at Unilever PLC, including Chief Digital Transformation Officer and Chief Operating Officer. His extensive background in overseeing large-scale operations, driving digital initiatives, and navigating complex market dynamics is expected to significantly bolster Infosys’ strategic decision-making and operational excellence as the company continues to expand its global footprint and service offerings.

The newly created Vice Chairman position is designed to provide enhanced strategic support to the board and the executive management team. This role is particularly crucial as Infosys focuses on leveraging emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, cloud computing, and advanced analytics to deliver transformative solutions for its diverse international client base. Mr. Paranjpe's insights from the consumer goods sector, combined with his digital transformation expertise, are anticipated to contribute valuable perspectives to Infosys' ambitious growth strategy.

Chairman Nandan Nilekani’s accompanying declaration was made to address and clarify any potential speculation regarding future leadership transitions. He explicitly stated that he continues to serve as the Non-executive Chairman of Infosys and emphasized that there are no plans in place for a change in this role in the foreseeable future. This statement is widely interpreted as a move to reassure investors and stakeholders about the stability of the company’s senior leadership. Mr. Nilekani, a co-founder of Infosys, notably returned to lead the board as Non-executive Chairman in August 2017 during a period of leadership transition and has since been instrumental in guiding the company's strategic resurgence and governance framework.

The dual announcement of Mr. Paranjpe’s appointment and Mr. Nilekani’s commitment reinforces Infosys’ robust corporate governance structure. The enhanced board leadership is poised to support the company’s ongoing efforts in innovation, market expansion, and talent development. As the technology sector continues to evolve rapidly, maintaining a stable and experienced leadership team is critical for navigating challenges and capitalizing on new opportunities. Infosys remains focused on executing its long-term strategy, delivering sustainable value to its shareholders, and maintaining its position as a global leader in IT services under its established leadership.
