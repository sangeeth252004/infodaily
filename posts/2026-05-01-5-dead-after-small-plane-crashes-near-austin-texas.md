A single-engine plane crashed near Austin, Texas, on Sunday, May 26, 2024, resulting in the deaths of all five individuals on board. The incident occurred in Burnet County, north of Austin, and involved a Cessna 210 aircraft. Authorities have initiated an investigation into the cause of the fatal accident.

The aircraft had departed from Houston-Hobby Airport (HOU) and was en route to Burnet Municipal Airport (KBMQ) when it crashed. The pilot and four passengers were confirmed deceased at the scene. Emergency services, including the Burnet County Sheriff's Office, responded to reports of the crash. The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) was quickly notified and commenced an initial assessment of the site.

According to preliminary reports and witness accounts, the plane experienced mechanical issues shortly before the crash. Witnesses in the area reported hearing the aircraft's engine sputtering and observed the plane descending rapidly. The wreckage was found scattered over a debris field, indicating the severity of the impact.

*   **Aircraft Type:** Cessna 210 (single-engine)
*   **Occupants:** Five individuals (pilot and four passengers)
*   **Origin:** Houston-Hobby Airport (HOU)
*   **Destination:** Burnet Municipal Airport (KBMQ)
*   **Location of Crash:** Burnet County, north of Austin, Texas
*   **Date:** Sunday, May 26, 2024
*   **Agencies Investigating:** Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) and National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB)

The National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) has taken over as the lead investigative agency. NTSB investigators are responsible for determining the probable cause of the accident, a process that can take several months to over a year. Their investigation will involve examining the aircraft's maintenance records, pilot's flight history, air traffic control communications, weather conditions at the time of the crash, and physical evidence from the wreckage.

Local law enforcement agencies have secured the crash site to preserve evidence for the NTSB's comprehensive examination. Further details regarding the identities of the deceased have not been officially released pending family notification. The investigation remains ongoing, with authorities committed to providing transparent updates as information becomes available.
