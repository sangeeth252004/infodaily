Patna, Bihar – The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has officially opened the application window for its 72nd Combined Competitive Examination (CCE) 2026. The announcement, released recently, details a revised total of 1186 vacancies across various departments within the Bihar state administration, providing a significant recruitment opportunity for aspiring civil servants. Interested candidates can apply online through the BPSC's official website, bpsc.bih.nic.in.

This iteration of the Combined Competitive Examination is designated for the 2026 cycle and seeks to fill critical administrative and executive positions. The revision in the total number of vacancies to 1186 indicates adjustments made by the commission since initial projections, reflecting current governmental staffing needs. These roles are integral to the functioning of various state departments, including administrative services, police services, revenue services, and other specialized cadres crucial for public service delivery.

Key details regarding the application process and eligibility criteria have been outlined in the comprehensive official notification.
*   **Application Period:** The online application process recently commenced, with specific start and end dates provided in the official advertisement. Candidates are advised to consult the BPSC website for the precise application window and deadlines.
*   **Eligibility:** Applicants are generally required to hold a bachelor's degree from a recognized university. Age limits and other specific educational qualifications vary depending on the post and category, detailed thoroughly in the official notification.
*   **Selection Process:** The 72nd CCE typically involves a multi-stage selection process designed to assess candidates comprehensively. This includes a Preliminary Examination (objective type), a Main Examination (written subjective type), and a Personality Test (interview).
*   **Vacancy Distribution:** While the total revised vacancies stand at 1186, the breakdown of posts per department and category is available in the detailed notification, allowing candidates to identify relevant opportunities.

The BPSC Combined Competitive Examination holds substantial importance for career aspirants in Bihar, serving as the primary gateway to esteemed positions within the state's public sector. The rigorous selection process aims to recruit competent individuals who will contribute to governance and public service delivery across the state. This recruitment drive is expected to attract a large number of applicants from across Bihar and neighboring regions, underscoring the demand for public sector employment.
