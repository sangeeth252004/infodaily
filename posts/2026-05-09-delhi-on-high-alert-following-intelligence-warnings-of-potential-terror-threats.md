New Delhi has been placed on a heightened security alert following specific warnings from intelligence agencies regarding potential terror attacks targeting the national capital. The advisory has prompted an immediate and significant augmentation of security measures across Delhi, with a particular focus on the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) Headquarters and other critical installations.

The intelligence inputs, shared with Delhi Police and other security establishments, indicate a credible threat of terrorist activity. In response, law enforcement agencies have initiated a comprehensive security review and deployment strategy to mitigate risks and ensure public safety. The directive emphasizes increased vigilance and preparedness, especially in politically sensitive areas and high-footfall zones.

Key details of the enhanced security protocols include:

*   **BJP Headquarters Security:** Security around the BJP’s central office on Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Marg has been notably bolstered. This includes the deployment of additional armed personnel, enhanced barricading, and stricter access controls for vehicles and pedestrians. The perimeter is under continuous surveillance.
*   **City-wide Vigilance:** The alert extends beyond the BJP HQ to encompass other vital government buildings, diplomatic enclaves, commercial hubs, markets, railway stations, bus terminals, and airports. Security forces are maintaining a strong presence in these areas, with particular attention to crowded public spaces.
*   **Intensified Patrolling and Checks:** Delhi Police has intensified patrolling, particularly in sensitive zones and border areas connecting the National Capital Region. Vehicle checks and verification of suspicious individuals have been ramped up. Special teams are conducting intelligence-based operations to pre-empt any untoward incidents.
*   **Technological Surveillance:** Advanced surveillance technology, including drones and an expanded network of CCTV cameras, is being utilized to monitor public spaces and critical infrastructure round-the-clock. Real-time data analysis is informing deployment decisions and potential threat identification.
*   **Inter-Agency Coordination:** Multiple security agencies, including the Delhi Police Special Cell, anti-terror units, and central intelligence bodies, are working in close coordination. Information sharing and joint operational planning are central to the current security strategy to ensure a unified response.

Sources within the security establishment indicate that the threat warnings are being taken with utmost seriousness given the current geopolitical landscape and past instances of terror plots targeting major Indian cities. The BJP Headquarters, as a prominent political establishment, is often considered a symbolic target, leading to its specific mention in the security advisory.

Authorities have confirmed that the enhanced security measures will remain in effect until further assessment of the threat situation. The public is advised to cooperate with security personnel and adhere to all guidelines issued for safety and security. Regular updates from official channels are expected as the situation evolves, with security forces committed to maintaining peace and order in the capital.
