Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU) has officially released the revised date sheet for its June 2026 Term End Examinations (TEE). The updated schedule, which details the examination period, is now available for students preparing for their academic assessments. The examinations are slated to commence on June 1, 2026, and conclude on July 21, 2026, providing a structured timeframe for all registered candidates across various programs.

The revised date sheet is a critical document for thousands of IGNOU students enrolled in a wide array of undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and certificate programs. This updated schedule supersedes any previously circulated timelines, offering definitive clarity regarding the examination calendar. Students are advised to download the official PDF document directly from the university's designated examination portal on its website to ensure they have access to the most current and accurate information. The June session represents a significant assessment period for a vast cohort of distance learners operating within India and internationally.

According to the newly released schedule, examinations will be conducted in two distinct shifts each day. The morning session is scheduled to run from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM, while the afternoon session will take place from 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM. This dual-shift system is a standard operational practice for IGNOU, designed to efficiently accommodate the large volume of students and the diverse range of course offerings. The specific dates and timings for each course code are meticulously detailed within the revised date sheet, necessitating that students carefully identify their respective examination slots.

Students preparing for the June 2026 TEE are reminded of several key requirements necessary for examination eligibility. These commonly include the timely submission of all required assignments for their respective courses by the stipulated deadlines, successful re-registration for their current academic year or semester, and holding valid enrollment for the specific courses they intend to appear for. Failure to fulfill these prerequisites may result in students being ineligible to sit for their examinations. The release of this revised schedule underscores the importance of proactive preparation and adherence to established university guidelines.

Key details regarding the June 2026 TEE are as follows:
*   **Examination Period:** June 1, 2026, to July 21, 2026.
*   **Availability:** Accessible for download on the official IGNOU examination website.
*   **Examination Shifts:**
    *   Morning Session: 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM
    *   Evening Session: 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM
*   **Affected Programs:** Covers various undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and certificate programs offered by the university.
*   **Student Action:** Candidates must cross-verify their program-specific and course-specific examination dates and times against the published schedule.

The university typically releases admit cards, also known as hall tickets, approximately one week to ten days prior to the commencement of the examinations. These admit cards will contain crucial information, including the student's enrollment number, the address of their allocated examination center, and the specific dates and timings of their scheduled exams. Students are strongly encouraged to regularly visit the official IGNOU website for further updates regarding the admit card release and any other pertinent announcements related to the June 2026 Term End Examinations. A proactive approach in monitoring official communications will enable students to remain fully informed and to plan their final preparations effectively.
