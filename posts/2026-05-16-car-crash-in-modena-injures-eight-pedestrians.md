Eight pedestrians sustained injuries after a car driven by an 84-year-old man crashed into a crowd near a supermarket in Modena, Italy, on Saturday, May 25, 2024. The incident occurred on Via Emilia Est, a significant thoroughfare in the city, prompting an immediate emergency response from local authorities and medical personnel.

The collision resulted in eight individuals being hospitalized with varying degrees of injury. One person was reported to be in serious condition, while the remaining seven sustained less severe injuries. All injured parties were transported to local medical facilities for treatment following the incident. Emergency services, including multiple ambulances and police units, quickly arrived at the scene to provide assistance and secure the area.

Preliminary reports indicate the driver, whose identity has not been fully released, was hospitalized in a state of shock following the crash. Local law enforcement has initiated a full investigation into the precise circumstances that led to the vehicle striking the pedestrians. Investigators are examining all potential factors, including the possibility of driver error, such as mistaking the accelerator for the brake pedal, though no official cause has been confirmed.

*   **Date:** Saturday, May 25, 2024
*   **Location:** Near a supermarket on Via Emilia Est, Modena, Italy

The incident temporarily disrupted traffic flow on Via Emilia Est as emergency crews worked to clear the scene and manage the aftermath. Authorities have appealed for witnesses to come forward with any information that could aid the ongoing investigation. The focus remains on understanding how the vehicle lost control and the extent of the impact on those injured.
