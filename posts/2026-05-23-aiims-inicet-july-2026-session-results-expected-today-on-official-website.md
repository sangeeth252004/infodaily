The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) is expected to release the results for the Institute of National Importance Combined Entrance Test (INICET) July 2026 session today, June 14, 2026. Aspiring medical postgraduates who appeared for the examination will be able to access their scores and ranks on the official AIIMS website, aiimsexams.ac.in, once the results are published.

INICET serves as the gateway for admissions into various postgraduate medical courses, including Doctor of Medicine (MD), Master of Surgery (MS), Master of Chirurgie (M.Ch), Doctor of Medicine (DM), and Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) programs. These programs are offered at AIIMS institutions nationwide, along with other prominent medical institutes such as JIPMER Puducherry, PGIMER Chandigarh, NIMHANS Bengaluru, and SCTIMST Thiruvananthapuram. The July 2026 session examination was conducted earlier this month, on June 2, 2026, as a computer-based test (CBT) across multiple centers in India.

The release of these results marks a significant phase for thousands of medical graduates seeking to advance their careers in specialized fields. The merit list will detail the qualifying candidates' roll numbers, percentile scores, and the categories under which they have qualified. Candidates are advised to keep their registration ID and password ready to access their individual scorecards.

Following the result declaration, the next critical step for qualified candidates will be the counselling process. AIIMS typically conducts multiple rounds of online counselling for seat allocation based on the INICET rank, candidate preferences, and seat availability in various participating institutes and specialties. Detailed schedules for the counselling rounds, including dates for registration, choice filling, and seat allocation, are expected to be announced on the AIIMS official portal in the coming weeks. Candidates are strongly advised to regularly check the official website for updates to ensure they do not miss any crucial deadlines.

To check the INICET July 2026 session results, candidates should follow these steps:
*   Visit the official AIIMS examination website: aiimsexams.ac.in.
*   Navigate to the "Recruitment" or "Results" section on the homepage.
*   Click on the link specifically designated for "Result of INICET July 2026 Session."
*   Candidates may be prompted to enter their login credentials, such as registration ID, RUC (Registration Unique Code), and password.
*   The result scorecard will be displayed on the screen.
*   It is recommended to download and print the scorecard for future reference and for the upcoming counselling process.

The AIIMS administration has not yet specified an exact time for the result declaration, but candidates are encouraged to monitor the official website throughout the day. Successful candidates will proceed to a rigorous counselling and verification process designed to ensure fair and transparent admissions to highly competitive postgraduate medical programs across India.
