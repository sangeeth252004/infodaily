The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has issued an official announcement detailing the process for candidates to check their scores for the REET Mains 2026 Level 1 and Level 2 examinations. The Board confirmed that results, including individual marks and merit lists, will be made available exclusively on its official website, providing a clear pathway for the lakhs of aspirants awaiting their outcomes. While a precise date for the result publication was not specified in the initial communication, the announcement indicates their imminent release.

The REET (Rajasthan Eligibility Examination for Teachers) Mains exam is a critical state-level assessment for individuals seeking to qualify for various teaching positions across government schools in Rajasthan. The examination is bifurcated into two levels: Level 1 for prospective primary school teachers (Classes 1-5) and Level 2 for upper primary school teachers (Classes 6-8). Successful qualification in these examinations is a prerequisite for subsequent recruitment processes conducted by the state government, making the release of these results a pivotal moment for a significant number of job seekers.

The REET Mains 2026 examinations were conducted by the RSSB across multiple centres throughout Rajasthan during February 2026. The examination cycle aimed to fill thousands of teaching vacancies, attracting widespread participation from candidates across the state. The announcement of result checking details follows a rigorous evaluation process and marks a crucial step in the state's teacher recruitment drive. The transparency in providing a clear method for result access aims to streamline the post-examination phase for all candidates.

The publication of these results will determine the eligibility of candidates for the next stages of the recruitment process, which typically involve document verification and subsequent counselling rounds. For those who meet the cut-off criteria, it signifies progression towards securing a government teaching role, which offers stability and public service opportunities. The RSSB's communication emphasizes the importance of relying solely on the official portal for result-related information to avoid misinformation.

Key details regarding checking the REET Mains 2026 results are as follows:

*   **Official Website:** Candidates must visit the official website of the Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) at `https://rssb.rajasthan.gov.in`.
*   **Result Link:** A dedicated link for "REET Mains 2026 Level 1 Result" and "REET Mains 2026 Level 2 Result" will be activated on the homepage.
*   **Required Credentials:** To access their individual scorecards, candidates will typically need to enter their examination roll number and date of birth or other registration details as prompted on the result portal.
*   **Merit List:** Alongside individual results, the RSSB is expected to release comprehensive merit lists, categorised by level and reservation criteria, detailing the performance of all appeared candidates.
*   **Expected Release:** While an exact date remains unconfirmed, the announcement from RSSB suggests that results are expected to be published shortly after the notification.

Following the declaration of results, qualified candidates will be advised to monitor the RSSB website for further instructions regarding document verification schedules and subsequent counselling processes. These next steps are essential for the final selection and appointment of successful candidates to teaching positions within Rajasthan's education system. The RSSB is committed to ensuring a fair and transparent recruitment cycle from examination to final placement.
