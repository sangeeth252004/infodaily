Atal Bihari Vajpayee Medical University (ABVMU) has officially released the admit cards for the Uttar Pradesh Common Paramedical Entrance Test (UP CNET) 2026. Candidates registered for the upcoming examination can now access and download their hall tickets directly from the university's dedicated portal, abvmucet26.co.in. The release of these admit cards is a crucial step for applicants intending to pursue various paramedical courses across institutions affiliated with ABVMU.

The UP CNET is a state-level entrance examination conducted annually by ABVMU to facilitate admissions into a range of Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) Paramedical courses. These programs include B.Optom, B.R.I.T, BMLT, BPT, and BOT, among others. The admit card serves as a mandatory document for entry into the examination hall, verifying a candidate's eligibility and providing essential details such as the exam center, date, and time.

To download their admit cards, candidates are required to log in to the official website using their registration ID and date of birth. It is imperative for all applicants to download and print a physical copy of their hall ticket well in advance of the examination date.

Key information and steps for candidates:
*   **Official Website:** Access the admit card download link at abvmucet26.co.in.
*   **Required Credentials:** Candidates must use their unique Registration ID and Date of Birth to log in.
*   **Verification:** Upon downloading, candidates should meticulously verify all details printed on the admit card, including their name, photograph, signature, roll number, examination center address, date, and time of the test.
*   **Discrepancies:** In case of any discrepancies or errors on the admit card, candidates are advised to contact the ABVMU examination authority immediately for rectification.
*   **Mandatory Documents:** On the day of the examination, candidates must carry a printed copy of their admit card along with a valid photo identification document (e.g., Aadhar card, Passport, Driving License, Voter ID).
*   **Prohibited Items:** Strict guidelines prohibit carrying electronic devices, study materials, or any unauthorized items into the examination hall. Candidates should review the instructions provided on the admit card carefully.

The UP CNET 2026 examination is scheduled to take place as per the dates mentioned on the admit cards. Candidates are urged to familiarize themselves with the examination pattern and syllabus, ensuring adequate preparation. Adherence to all instructions and regulations specified by ABVMU is crucial for a smooth examination process. Further updates regarding the entrance test will be communicated via the official university website.
