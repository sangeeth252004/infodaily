The Gujarat State Road Transport Corporation (GSRTC) has officially initiated its recruitment drive for 4,599 driver positions, with online registration commencing recently. This significant hiring initiative aims to strengthen the corporation's workforce and enhance its operational capabilities ahead of the 2026 service cycle. Prospective candidates interested in contributing to public transportation across Gujarat are invited to apply through the official GSRTC portal.

This recruitment drive represents a substantial opportunity for job seekers within Gujarat, particularly those with the requisite driving qualifications and experience. The addition of nearly 4,600 new drivers is expected to significantly bolster the efficiency and coverage of GSRTC's extensive bus network, which serves millions of commuters daily across urban and rural areas of the state. The corporation plays a crucial role in inter-city and intra-city connectivity, facilitating economic activity and social mobility throughout Gujarat.

According to the official announcement, the application process is entirely online. Candidates are advised to carefully review the detailed notification available on the GSRTC's designated recruitment webpage. This notification outlines the comprehensive eligibility criteria, which typically include age limits, educational qualifications, valid heavy vehicle driving licenses, and a specified period of driving experience. Required documents, application fees, and instructions for submission are also provided in the official release.

Key details of the GSRTC Driver Recruitment 2026 include:
*   **Organisation:** Gujarat State Road Transport Corporation (GSRTC)
*   **Positions Offered:** Driver
*   **Total Vacancies:** 4,599 posts
*   **Application Mode:** Online only
*   **Application Period:** Specific start and end dates for online registration are detailed in the official notification.
*   **Information Source:** Official GSRTC recruitment portal, where a direct link and detailed steps to apply are available.
*   **Eligibility:** Candidates must meet specific criteria regarding age, educational background, and possessing a valid heavy vehicle driving license, as specified in the official advertisement.

The recruitment process is anticipated to involve multiple stages, typically including a written examination, a practical driving test to assess skills and road safety knowledge, document verification, and a medical examination. Successful candidates will undergo further training as part of their onboarding to meet GSRTC's operational standards and service expectations. This structured approach ensures that only qualified and competent individuals are inducted into the corporation's driving fleet.

The completion of this recruitment drive is expected to have a positive impact on public transport infrastructure in Gujarat, allowing GSRTC to potentially expand its routes, increase bus frequency on existing services, and improve overall passenger experience. For eligible applicants, this presents a stable employment opportunity with a state-run corporation, contributing directly to the vital public service sector. Candidates are encouraged to apply well before any stated deadlines to avoid last-minute technical issues. Further updates regarding the selection process will be communicated through official channels by GSRTC.
