An Indian Army Captain died on Saturday, June 15, 2024, after sustaining fatal injuries during an anti-terror operation in the Rajouri district of Jammu and Kashmir. The officer, whose identity has not yet been officially released, reportedly fell into a deep gorge while engaged in military action against a group of suspected terrorists in a dense forest area.

The operation was initiated in the challenging Kalal forest area of Rajouri following specific intelligence inputs regarding the presence and movement of militant elements. Security forces launched a coordinated cordon and search operation, a standard procedure designed to locate and neutralize threats in such environments. During the course of this intense engagement, the Captain, who was actively leading a patrol, encountered unforeseen hazards inherent to the mountainous and heavily forested terrain. Preliminary reports suggest he fell into a deep gorge, leading to critical injuries to which he later succumbed.

This incident highlights the significant and persistent dangers faced by Indian security personnel deployed in counter-terrorism operations across the Union Territory. The Pir Panjal range, which encompasses the Rajouri and Poonch districts, has become a focal point for increased anti-terror activities in recent years. Militant groups often exploit the dense forests, difficult topography, and natural hideouts to establish presence and carry out subversive acts. The Indian Army and other security agencies are engaged in continuous efforts to dismantle these networks and maintain regional stability.

*   **Location of Operation:** Kalal forest area, Rajouri district, Jammu and Kashmir.
*   **Date of Incident:** Saturday, June 15, 2024.
*   **Reported Cause of Death:** Falling into a deep gorge during the operation.
*   **Operational Context:** Intelligence-led counter-terrorism drive against suspected militants.
*   **Current Status:** Search operations are ongoing in the area.

Following the tragedy, search operations in the Kalal forest area intensified significantly to track down the terrorists. Additional contingents of security forces have been deployed, expanding the cordon to ensure no militant elements can escape the area. This robust response underscores the unwavering resolve of the Indian Army to continue its counter-terrorism efforts despite the operational challenges and sacrifices involved. Local authorities have not yet released further details regarding the ongoing search or any potential contact with the remaining militants. The incident serves as a solemn reminder of the commitment and risks undertaken by military personnel safeguarding national security in sensitive regions.
