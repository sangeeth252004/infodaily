The incident, which reportedly occurred this week, involves a BJP neta from the Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir. According to the leader's statement, attempts to secure lodging at a hotel in Maharashtra were unsuccessful, with staff allegedly citing the individual's Kashmiri background as the reason for denial. Specific details regarding the name of the leader or the precise location of the hotel were not immediately made public in initial reports, but the core allegation centers on an experience of prejudice.
