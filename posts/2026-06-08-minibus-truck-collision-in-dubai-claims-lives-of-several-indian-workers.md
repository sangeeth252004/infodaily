The employer expressed deep condolences to the families of the deceased and stated that measures are being taken to provide support to those affected.

*   The collision occurred in Dubai.

*   While the exact number of fatalities and injured individuals has not been publicly detailed in the immediate announcement, reports confirm "several" deaths.
