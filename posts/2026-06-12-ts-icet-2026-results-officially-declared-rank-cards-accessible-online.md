HYDERABAD – The results for the Telangana State Integrated Common Entrance Test (TS ICET) 2026 have been officially declared by Kakatiya University, Warangal, on behalf of the Telangana State Council of Higher Education (TSCHE). Candidates who appeared for the entrance examination can now access and download their rank cards directly from the official portal, icet.tgche.ac.in. The declaration marks a significant step for thousands of aspirants seeking admission to Master of Business Administration (MBA) and Master of Computer Applications (MCA) programmes across various universities and affiliated colleges in Telangana for the academic year 2026-27.

The TS ICET is a computer-based test conducted annually for admission into two-year full-time MBA and MCA programmes. For the 2026 cycle, the examination was conducted earlier this year, assessing candidates on their analytical ability, mathematical ability, and communication skills. The timely release of results ensures that the subsequent admission process can proceed as scheduled, allowing qualified candidates to prepare for the counseling rounds.

Students can download their rank cards by entering their registration number, hall ticket number, and date of birth on the specified result link available on the official website. The rank card is a crucial document, detailing the candidate's score, rank, and qualifying status. It is mandatory for participation in the forthcoming counseling sessions.

Key details regarding accessing the TS ICET 2026 Rank Card:

*   **Official Website:** icet.tgche.ac.in
*   **Required Credentials:** Candidate's Registration Number, TS ICET Hall Ticket Number, and Date of Birth.
*   **Information Displayed:** The rank card will include the candidate's name, parent's name, roll number, section-wise marks, total marks obtained, overall rank, and qualifying status.
*   **Significance:** The rank card is a prerequisite for the counseling process. Candidates are advised to download and secure multiple printouts for future reference.

Thousands of candidates appeared for the TS ICET 2026, competing for limited seats in prestigious institutions across the state. The qualifying marks for TS ICET are generally set at 25% (50 marks out of 200) for general category candidates. There are no minimum qualifying marks for candidates belonging to Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes. The ranks obtained in this examination are crucial for determining admission eligibility and seat allocation based on merit and reservation criteria.

Following the result declaration, the TSCHE is expected to announce the detailed schedule for the TS ICET 2026 counseling process. The counseling typically involves registration, document verification, choice filling, seat allotment, and reporting to the allotted colleges. Candidates are advised to regularly check the official TS ICET website and the TSCHE portal for updates on counseling dates, required documents, and procedure details to ensure a smooth admission journey for the 2026-27 academic year.
