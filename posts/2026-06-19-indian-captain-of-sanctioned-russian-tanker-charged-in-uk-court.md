The UK has been a vocal proponent of strict adherence to international sanctions and has taken steps to monitor and intercept vessels suspected of non-compliance within its jurisdiction or international waters where it has authority. This case highlights the complexities and risks faced by seafarers employed on vessels linked to sanctioned operations.
