Nandani Bosmiya, a former candidate for the Aam Aadmi Party (AAP), was recently found dead in her residential flat in Rajkot, Gujarat. The incident has led to a police investigation after her family formally alleged that she was murdered by her live-in partner. Authorities have initiated proceedings to ascertain the circumstances surrounding her demise.

The discovery of Ms. Bosmiya's body has prompted significant local attention, primarily due to her political background and the grave allegations made by her family. Police officials in Rajkot confirmed that a case has been registered based on the family’s complaint. The focus of the preliminary investigation centers on establishing the exact cause of death and gathering evidence to corroborate or refute the claims of foul play.

Key details surrounding the incident and the ongoing investigation include:
*   **Identity of the Deceased:** Nandani Bosmiya, known for her prior association with the Aam Aadmi Party as a political candidate.
*   **Location:** The incident occurred at her residential flat located within Rajkot city.
*   **Allegation:** Her family has explicitly accused her live-in partner of murder, asserting their belief that Ms. Bosmiya's death was not natural or accidental.
*   **Police Action:** Following the family's complaint, law enforcement agencies have launched a full-scale investigation. This typically involves crime scene analysis, collection of forensic evidence, and initial questioning of individuals connected to the deceased.

The seriousness of a homicide allegation, particularly involving a public figure and an intimate relationship, underscores the meticulous nature of the police inquiry. Investigators are expected to conduct a comprehensive examination, including an autopsy to determine the precise medical cause of death. Forensic experts will analyze evidence from the flat to reconstruct the events leading up to Ms. Bosmiya's death.

Authorities have confirmed that statements are being recorded from family members and other potential witnesses who might possess information pertinent to the case. The individual named in the family's complaint, Ms. Bosmiya's live-in partner, is expected to be a central figure in the ongoing questioning as part of the standard investigative protocol. The police aim to gather all available information before drawing any conclusions or making any arrests.

As the investigation progresses, the Rajkot police department is expected to provide further updates. The legal framework surrounding such cases mandates a thorough and impartial inquiry, ensuring that all aspects are investigated without prejudice.
