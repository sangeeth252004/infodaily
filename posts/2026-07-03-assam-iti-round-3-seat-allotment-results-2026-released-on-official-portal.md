The Directorate of Employment and Craftsmen Training (DECT), Assam, has officially released the Round 3 seat allotment results for admissions to Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs) for the academic session 2026. Aspiring candidates who participated in the online counseling process can now access their provisional allotment letters through the official admission portal, itiassam.admissions.nic.in. This announcement marks a crucial stage for thousands of applicants seeking to enroll in various vocational and technical trades offered across the state's ITIs.

The release of the third round results aims to fill remaining vacant seats after the conclusion of the first two phases of counseling. Candidates who have been allotted a seat in this round are required to download their provisional allotment letter and complete the subsequent admission formalities within a specified timeframe. Failure to report to the designated ITI and submit the necessary documents by the stipulated deadline will result in the forfeiture of the allotted seat, making it available for subsequent admission processes if any.

ITIs play a significant role in Assam's educational landscape by providing practical, skill-based training that prepares students for immediate employment in various industries. These institutions offer a diverse range of engineering and non-engineering trades, contributing to the state's skilled workforce and aligning with national initiatives for skill development. The online admission process, managed by DECT Assam, ensures transparency and accessibility for applicants from across the region.

To check their Round 3 seat allotment results, candidates must follow a structured procedure:

*   Visit the official website: itiassam.admissions.nic.in.
*   Locate and click on the specific link for "Round 3 Seat Allotment Result 2026" or a similar notification on the homepage.
*   Log in using their registered application number, password, and date of birth as credentials.
*   Upon successful login, the provisional allotment letter, detailing the assigned ITI and trade, will be displayed.
*   Candidates are advised to download and print this letter for all future admission-related correspondences and physical reporting.

Following the download of the allotment letter, successful candidates must report to their respective allotted ITIs for document verification and fee payment. This phase is mandatory to confirm the admission. Essential documents typically required for verification include:

*   Original and self-attested copies of educational qualification certificates and mark sheets (e.g., Class 10th, 12th).
*   Transfer Certificate (TC) from the last attended institution.
*   Caste Certificate (for SC/ST/OBC candidates, if applicable).
*   Permanent Resident Certificate (PRC) or Domicile Certificate.
*   Recent passport-sized photographs.
*   Medical Fitness Certificate.
*   Any other relevant certificates mentioned in the official prospectus or allotment letter.

The DECT Assam has set a strict schedule for these post-allotment procedures to ensure the timely commencement of the academic session 2026. Candidates are strongly advised to refer to the official website and their allotment letter for precise dates and instructions regarding reporting, document submission, and fee payment deadlines. The completion of this round finalizes the majority of admissions for the current academic year, paving the way for new batches of students to begin their vocational training journey.
