The State Council of Educational Research and Training (SCERT) Assam has officially released the admit cards for the Diploma in Elementary Education (DElEd) Pre-Entry Test (PET) 2026. Candidates who have applied for the DElEd course for the academic session 2026-2028 can now download their hall tickets from the designated official portal, scertpet.co.in. The release of these admit cards is a crucial step for applicants preparing to sit for the examination, which serves as a gateway to elementary teacher training programs across the state.

The DElEd PET is a mandatory examination for individuals aspiring to pursue a career as elementary school teachers in Assam. The admit card is a vital document, confirming a candidate's eligibility and providing essential details about the upcoming test. Without a valid admit card, candidates will not be permitted to enter the examination hall. This development directly impacts a significant number of prospective educators in Assam, streamlining their access to the next stage of the admission process.

To download the admit card, candidates are required to follow a straightforward procedure:

*   **Visit the Official Website**: Navigate to scertpet.co.in.
*   **Locate Admit Card Link**: Find the specific link for downloading the DElEd PET 2026 admit card, typically highlighted on the homepage.
*   **Enter Credentials**: Provide the necessary login details, which typically include the application number and date of birth.
*   **Download and Print**: Once submitted, the admit card will be displayed. Candidates must download it and take a printout for future reference and examination day use.

The admit card contains critical information such as the candidate's name, roll number, photograph, signature, examination date, time, and the allotted test centre venue. Applicants are advised to meticulously verify all details printed on their admit cards to ensure accuracy. In case of any discrepancies, candidates should promptly contact the SCERT Assam authorities for rectification before the examination date.

The DElEd program is a two-year diploma course designed to train teachers for elementary levels (Classes I to VIII) in various schools. The successful completion of this program is often a prerequisite for appointment as an elementary teacher in the state's public education system. The PET 2026 plays a pivotal role in selecting eligible candidates for these training programs, thereby contributing to the quality of elementary education in Assam.

Following the examination, SCERT Assam will proceed with the evaluation of answer sheets and subsequent declaration of results. Successful candidates will then typically participate in a counseling process for admission to various DElEd institutions across the state. Further details regarding the examination schedule, result declaration, and counseling process will be communicated by SCERT Assam through official channels.
