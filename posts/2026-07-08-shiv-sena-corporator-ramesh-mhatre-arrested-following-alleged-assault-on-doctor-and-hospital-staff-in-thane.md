Shiv Sena corporator Ramesh Mhatre was arrested in Thane, Maharashtra, following allegations of assault against a doctor and hospital staff members. The arrest occurred after a video purportedly showing the incident circulated widely online, drawing significant public attention and strong condemnation from various sectors. Mhatre, a political representative from the Shiv Sena party, now faces charges related to the alleged physical altercation that took place at a medical facility in Thane.

The incident gained prominence after the video footage, depicting a man identified as Mhatre physically confronting and assaulting a doctor and members of the hospital staff, spread across social media platforms. Initial reports indicate the altercation stemmed from a dispute within the hospital premises. The exact circumstances leading up to the alleged assault remain under investigation, but the visual evidence played a critical role in generating widespread outrage and prompting swift action from law enforcement authorities.

The alleged assault on healthcare professionals by a public figure has reignited concerns about the safety and security of medical personnel. Doctors and hospital staff frequently report incidents of verbal abuse and physical violence while performing their duties, making this case particularly impactful due to the involvement of an elected corporator. Medical associations and healthcare advocacy groups were among those who publicly condemned the incident, emphasizing the need for stricter measures to protect healthcare workers. The incident also sparked discussions among political circles regarding the conduct expected of public representatives.

Following the circulation of the video and the ensuing public pressure, Thane police initiated an investigation. Mhatre was subsequently apprehended and taken into custody. Charges have been filed against him under relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code, which typically include provisions for assault, criminal intimidation, and obstructing public servants from discharging their duties. The precise sections applied will be detailed as the investigation progresses.
