The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) has recently announced the results for the second round of seat allotment for the Institute of National Importance Combined Entrance Test (INI CET) for the 2026 academic session. Candidates who participated in the counselling process can now access their rank-wise allotment status directly on the official AIIMS examinations website, aiimsexams.ac.in. This crucial step determines admissions to various postgraduate medical and dental courses across premier institutions in India.

The INI CET examination facilitates admission to Doctor of Medicine (MD), Master of Surgery (MS), Master of Chirurgiae (M.Ch - 6 years), Doctor of Medicine (DM - 6 years), and Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) programs. These courses are offered at various Institutes of National Importance (INIs), including all AIIMS branches, Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education and Research (JIPMER) Puducherry, National Institute of Mental Health and Neurosciences (NIMHANS) Bengaluru, Postgraduate Institute of Medical Education and Research (PGIMER) Chandigarh, and Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences and Technology (SCTIMST) Thiruvananthapuram. The Round 2 allotment follows the merit-based selection process, taking into account candidate preferences and seat availability.

Following the publication of the Round 2 results, candidates are required to undertake several steps to secure their allotted seats. The official portal, aiimsexams.ac.in, provides comprehensive instructions for the subsequent admission process. This typically involves downloading the allotment letter, accepting the allocated seat, and reporting to the respective institution for document verification and fee submission. Specific deadlines for these actions are detailed in the official information brochure and on the AIIMS examinations website.

Key details regarding the INI CET 2026 Round 2 seat allotment:
*   **Access Point:** Results are available for download on the official website, aiimsexams.ac.in.
*   **Information Provided:** The rank-wise list includes details such as the candidate's roll number, overall rank, percentile score, the institute to which they have been allotted, and the specific subject or specialty.
*   **Admission Process:** Candidates must log in to the portal using their credentials to view their individual allotment status and download the offer letter.
*   **Reporting:** Successful candidates are mandated to report to their assigned Institute of National Importance within the stipulated timeframe, bringing all necessary original documents for verification.

The release of the second round allotment is a significant phase, potentially filling remaining vacancies from the first round and offering opportunities to candidates who may not have secured their desired seat initially. Candidates who are satisfied with their Round 2 allotment must complete the acceptance and reporting procedures as per the official schedule. Those who wish to withdraw their admission or were not allotted a seat may await further announcements regarding subsequent counselling rounds, if applicable. All candidates are strongly advised to refer exclusively to aiimsexams.ac.in for official notifications and updates related to the INI CET 2026 admission cycle.
