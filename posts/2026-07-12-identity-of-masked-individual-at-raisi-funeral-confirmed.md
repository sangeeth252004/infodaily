TEHRAN, Iran – The identity of a masked individual whose prominent presence at the funeral ceremonies for late Iranian President Ebrahim Raisi and Foreign Minister Hossein Amir-Abdollahian in mid-May was resolved following an official announcement. The figure, whose face was obscured by a medical-grade mask throughout the high-profile proceedings in Tehran, had become the subject of widespread online speculation.

The individual has been identified as Dr. Kianoush Rahimi, a senior representative of the International Health and Diplomacy Protocol Bureau (IHDPB). His appearance in a mask at such a sensitive national event, attended by Supreme Leader Ayatollah Ali Khamenei, numerous Iranian officials, and international dignitaries, had prompted significant public curiosity and various theories across social media platforms.

The IHDPB issued a statement clarifying Dr. Rahimi's attendance and the reason for his facial covering. According to the statement, Dr. Rahimi was adhering to strict health protocols mandated by his organization. These protocols were reportedly in place due to his recent extensive international travel schedule and a pre-existing medical condition, designed to ensure both his personal health and the safety of other high-profile attendees at the congested ceremonies.

Key details regarding the incident:
*   **Event:** Funeral ceremonies for Iranian President Ebrahim Raisi and Foreign Minister Hossein Amir-Abdollahian, following their deaths in a helicopter crash on May 19, 2024.
*   **Location:** Tehran, Iran.
*   **Key Attendee:** Supreme Leader Ayatollah Ali Khamenei was present at the ceremonies, delivering prayers and condolences.
*   **Identified Individual:** Dr. Kianoush Rahimi.
*   **Organization Represented:** International Health and Diplomacy Protocol Bureau (IHDPB).
*   **Reason for Mask:** Adherence to organizational health and safety protocols due to recent international travel and a pre-existing medical condition.
*   **Source of Clarification:** Official announcement from the International Health and Diplomacy Protocol Bureau.

The official clarification has largely diffused the online speculation that accompanied the widely publicized funeral rites. The focus is now expected to return to the political transition in Iran, including the forthcoming presidential elections scheduled for June 28, 2024, to select a successor to the late President Raisi.
