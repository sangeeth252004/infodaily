On May 28, 2024, the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) issued a legal notice seeking ₹100 crore in damages from National Conference (NC) leader Omar Abdullah in Jammu and Kashmir. The notice was served following alleged defamatory statements made by Abdullah, claiming the BJP was attempting to "poach" elected Members of the Legislative Assembly (MLAs) in the region.

The legal action underscores an escalation in political tensions within the Union Territory. The BJP contends that Abdullah's claims are unsubstantiated and have caused significant damage to the party's public image and reputation. This development occurs amidst an active political landscape in Jammu and Kashmir, with discussions surrounding potential future assembly elections.

The notice, served through BJP's legal counsel Advocate Sunil Sethi, specifically references a press conference held by Mr. Abdullah in Srinagar. During this conference, Abdullah reportedly stated, "some of our MLAs and some of the PDP MLAs have been approached by BJP to join their party." The BJP has categorically denied these allegations, labeling the remarks as "false, baseless, and defamatory" and asserting they were made with malicious intent.

The legal notice outlines specific demands addressed to Mr. Abdullah:
*   Immediately withdraw the allegedly defamatory statement regarding MLA poaching.
*   Issue an unconditional public apology to the Bharatiya Janata Party.
*   Pay ₹100 crore in damages to the BJP within 30 days of receiving the notice.

The notice further states that failure to comply with these demands within the stipulated period will result in the initiation of appropriate civil and criminal proceedings against Mr. Abdullah by the BJP.

In response to the legal notice, Omar Abdullah confirmed its receipt. He publicly stated his refusal to withdraw his comments or issue an apology, indicating that he stands by his earlier statements. Abdullah has reportedly questioned the BJP's selective legal challenge, pointing to similar statements made by other political figures, and has expressed readiness to face any potential legal proceedings initiated against him.

This development sets the stage for a potential legal confrontation, further intensifying the political discourse in Jammu and Kashmir. The situation is expected to unfold over the coming weeks as the 30-day period stipulated in the legal notice progresses, with the possibility of judicial intervention shaping future political dynamics in the region.
