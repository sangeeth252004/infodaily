BENGALURU, Karnataka – The Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) is scheduled to release the Round 1 provisional seat allotment results for the Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) 2026 counselling process tomorrow. Aspirants seeking admission to various undergraduate professional courses across Karnataka will be able to access their results on the official KEA website, cetonline.karnataka.gov.in. This announcement marks a critical juncture for thousands of students aiming to secure seats in engineering, architecture, pharmacy, agriculture, and other professional disciplines for the academic year 2026-27.

The KCET examination serves as the gateway for admissions to a wide array of professional colleges within Karnataka, making the provisional seat allotment a highly anticipated event. This initial list provides candidates with a preliminary indication of the college and course they have been provisionally allocated based on their KCET merit rank, preferences, and seat availability. The transparency of this process, managed by the KEA, aims to ensure equitable distribution of seats among eligible candidates.

Upon the release of the provisional allotment list, candidates are required to log in to their respective portals using their application number and password. The system will display their assigned seat, if any. Following this, students will enter a crucial phase of decision-making, where they must exercise their options based on the provisional allotment. These options typically include accepting the allocated seat (Freezing), accepting and wishing to participate in further rounds for an upgrade (Upgrading), or rejecting the allocated seat. Failure to exercise an option within the stipulated timeframe may result in forfeiture of the allotted seat.

Key details regarding the KCET 2026 Round 1 provisional seat allotment process:

*   **Result Release Date:** Tomorrow, as per the official KEA announcement.
*   **Official Website:** cetonline.karnataka.gov.in is the sole platform for checking results and participating in subsequent counselling steps.
*   **Required Credentials:** Candidates will need their KCET 2026 application number and password to log in and view their provisional allotment status.
*   **Counselling Process:** The allotment is based on merit, candidate preferences submitted during the option entry phase, and applicable reservation policies.
*   **Affected Courses:** Admissions to B.E./B.Tech (Engineering), B.Arch (Architecture), B.Pharm/Pharm.D (Pharmacy), B.Sc (Agriculture), B.V.Sc & A.H. (Veterinary Science), and other professional courses in government, aided, and private institutions across Karnataka.

Following the option entry period, KEA will announce the dates for payment of fees for candidates who accept their seats and wish to complete the admission formalities. Those who choose to upgrade their seats or reject the provisional allotment will have opportunities in subsequent counselling rounds. Students are strongly advised to meticulously review the instructions and deadlines published on the KEA website to ensure compliance and avoid missing critical steps in their admission journey. The KEA will subsequently release a final seat allotment list and conduct further rounds of counselling if seats remain vacant after the initial rounds.
