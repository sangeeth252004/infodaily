Patna, Bihar – The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has officially announced the postponement of the 72nd Combined Preliminary Examination, which was originally slated to be held in 2026. The commission has indicated that a revised schedule for the examination will be communicated to candidates in the near future. This announcement affects a significant number of aspirants preparing for various administrative and civil service positions within the state of Bihar.

The decision to postpone the examination was made public through an official announcement from the BPSC. While specific reasons for the postponement were not detailed in the initial communication, the commission has advised candidates that updates regarding the new examination date would be released promptly via its official website. The 72nd Combined Preliminary Examination represents a crucial initial screening stage for individuals aspiring to join the state's public administration, encompassing a range of posts across different government departments.

This postponement marks a significant development for the thousands of candidates across Bihar and other regions who have been preparing for this highly competitive examination. The BPSC Combined Competitive Examination process is typically structured into three stages: Preliminary, Main, and Interview. The preliminary stage serves as a screening test designed to shortlist eligible candidates for the subsequent main examination. Unforeseen changes in examination schedules can influence candidates' preparation strategies and timelines, necessitating continuous monitoring of official updates.

The BPSC is the primary state agency responsible for conducting recruitment examinations for various Group A, Group B, and Group C posts within the Bihar state administration. These examinations are integral to filling critical vacancies and ensuring the efficient functioning of government departments across the state. The competitive nature of these exams often sees hundreds of thousands of applicants vying for a limited number of positions, making timely and clear communication from the commission vital.

Key details regarding the BPSC 72nd Combined Preliminary Examination postponement:
*   **Exam Name:** BPSC 72nd Combined Preliminary Examination
*   **Original Status:** Scheduled for 2026, exact date not specified
*   **Current Status:** Postponed indefinitely
*   **New Date Announcement:** To be communicated soon by the Bihar Public Service Commission
*   **Information Source:** Official BPSC announcement
*   **Candidate Action:** Regularly check the official BPSC website for the revised examination schedule and all related notifications.

Candidates are strongly advised to remain vigilant for official notifications released directly by the BPSC and to avoid relying on unofficial sources or speculative information regarding the examination schedule. The commission's official portal (bpsc.bih.nic.in) will serve as the sole authentic source for all forthcoming announcements and updates.

Aspirants are encouraged to maintain their preparation momentum while staying informed through the official BPSC website for the impending announcement of the new examination date. Further details, including any revised application window (if applicable) and updated admit card release dates, are expected to follow the announcement of the new preliminary exam schedule. The commission is anticipated to provide adequate notice to allow candidates to adjust their plans accordingly.
