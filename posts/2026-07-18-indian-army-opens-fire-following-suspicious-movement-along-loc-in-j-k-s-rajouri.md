The Indian Army initiated fire along the Line of Control (LoC) in Jammu and Kashmir's Rajouri district late Monday night after troops detected suspicious movement in the Kalal sector. The incident, reported by defence officials, occurred as surveillance systems and forward posts identified individuals attempting to approach the heavily guarded border.

According to preliminary reports, alert troops observed suspicious activity near the fence along the LoC. Following the standard operating procedures for potential infiltration attempts, the army personnel opened fire to neutralize any threat and prevent cross-border movement. The firing continued intermittently for a period as the area was secured and extensively monitored. No casualties or specific recoveries have been officially reported immediately following the initial engagement.

The incident underscores the persistent security challenges along the LoC, particularly in the Rajouri and Poonch sectors, which are known for their rugged terrain and historical susceptibility to infiltration bids. Security forces maintain a high state of vigilance to counter attempts by militants to cross into Indian territory.

Key details surrounding the incident include:
*   **Location:** Kalal sector, Rajouri district, Jammu and Kashmir.
*   **Time of Incident:** Late Monday night.
*   **Observation:** Suspicious movement detected by alert troops near the LoC fence.
*   **Response:** Indian Army personnel opened fire to intercept the movement.
*   **Objective:** To thwart any potential infiltration bid.
*   **Aftermath:** Search operations were reportedly launched in the area following the firing to ensure no infiltrators managed to cross or leave behind any material.

This incident is part of a broader context of ongoing counter-infiltration and counter-terrorism operations by the Indian Army and other security agencies in Jammu and Kashmir. The region remains under heightened security scrutiny, with advanced surveillance technologies and strengthened ground patrols deployed to monitor the LoC. Authorities continue to emphasize that any attempts to breach the border will be met with a firm response.

Following the reported firing, security forces have reportedly intensified area domination patrols and launched comprehensive search operations in the affected sector and its immediate vicinity. These operations aim to ensure the complete sanitization of the area and to ascertain if any individuals managed to retreat or leave behind any evidence. Further details are expected as the situation evolves and more information becomes available from official military sources. The security posture along the LoC remains robust to deter future infiltration attempts.
