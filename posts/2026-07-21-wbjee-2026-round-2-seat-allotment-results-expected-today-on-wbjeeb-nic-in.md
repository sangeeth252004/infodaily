Kolkata, India – The West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations Board (WBJEEB) is scheduled to release the results for the second round of seat allotment for WBJEE 2026 today. Aspirants who participated in the counselling process for undergraduate admissions in engineering, technology, pharmacy, and architecture courses across West Bengal are advised to visit the official website, wbjeeb.nic.in, to access their allotment status.

The release of the Round 2 seat allotment results marks a significant phase in the admission cycle for thousands of students seeking higher education opportunities in the state. Following the publication of these results, candidates will need to meticulously review their allotted seats and proceed with the subsequent steps, which typically include confirming acceptance, paying the seat acceptance fee, and completing the necessary document verification. The WBJEEB will also release a detailed reporting schedule outlining deadlines for these critical admission procedures.

The WBJEE is a state-level common entrance examination conducted annually by the WBJEEB for admission to various undergraduate professional courses. The counselling process, conducted in multiple rounds, facilitates the allocation of seats based on merit, candidate preferences, and seat availability. Round 2 is often a crucial stage for many candidates who may not have secured their preferred institution or course in the initial round, or for those who chose to upgrade their options.

Upon accessing their results on the portal, candidates will generally be presented with options to either 'freeze' their allotted seat, indicating satisfaction with the current allocation, or 'float,' signifying a desire to be considered for an upgrade in subsequent rounds while provisionally accepting the current seat. Irrespective of the choice, candidates are typically required to pay a non-refundable seat acceptance fee online and download a provisional seat allotment letter.

The subsequent steps for admitted candidates will primarily involve physical reporting to the allotted institution for document verification. This process is mandatory to confirm the provisional admission and includes submitting and verifying a range of academic and personal documents.

Key documents typically required for verification include:
*   WBJEE 2026 Rank Card
*   WBJEE 2026 Admit Card
*   Class 10th Admit Card/Birth Certificate (as age proof)
*   Class 10th Mark Sheet
*   Class 12th Mark Sheet
*   Domicile Certificate (for West Bengal domiciled candidates)
*   Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC-A/OBC-B) if applicable
*   PWD Certificate if applicable
*   Provisional Seat Allotment Letter
*   Receipt of Seat Acceptance Fee Payment

Candidates are strongly advised to refer to the official WBJEEB website, wbjeeb.nic.in, for the precise reporting schedule, specific document requirements, and any additional instructions issued by the board. Failure to adhere to the specified timelines and procedures could lead to the cancellation of the allotted seat. The conclusion of Round 2 will pave the way for potential further rounds, such as mop-up rounds, should seats remain vacant after the current phase of admissions.
